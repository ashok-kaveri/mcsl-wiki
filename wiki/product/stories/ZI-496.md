## Open Issues

1. **WooCommerce Bundled Products: 'Assembled Weight - Ignore' setting not honored** — When a WooCommerce product is a bundle (using the official Woo Bundled Products plugin) configured to use only the parent product's weight/dimensions, WSS sums all child item weights instead. ## User Story

As an **Australian diesel-appliance retailer who has been hand-entering box dimensions on every order for over two years** because WSS sums up child-item weights for bundled products even when WooCommerce is configured to use only the parent weight, I want **WSS to respect WooCommerce Bundled Products' Assembled Weight – Ignore setting** so the booked rate matches the cart-quoted rate, so that **I stop overpaying carriers hundreds of dollars per month on incorrectly-sized parcels and so my staff stops being a manual workaround for a setting the plugin already exposes**.

---

## Acceptance Criteria (Simple)

- [ ] When a Woo Bundled Product has 'Assembled Weight: Ignore' set, WSS uses only the parent product weight/dimensions for rate quoting AND label booking
- [ ] When 'Assembled Weight: Sum' is set, WSS sums the child items (current behavior preserved)
- [ ] Order #191081 on dieselheat.com.au (parent: Eberspacher D2L AS3 Diesel Air Heater Marine Kit) ships at 50×50×20 cm / 10 kg, not the summed weight
- [ ] Cart-quoted rate and booked-rate match for every bundled-product order — eliminating the documented quote-vs-book drift
- [ ] Customer can stop manually editing box dimensions for bundled products (verify on a sample week of orders)

---

## Acceptance Criteria (Given/When/Then)

### Scenario: Bundled product configured with 'Ignore Assembled Weight' ships at parent weight

- **Given** a WooCommerce store with WooCommerce Bundled Products installed and a bundle configured with 'Assembled Weight: Ignore' (parent weight 10 kg, parent dims 50×50×20)
- **When** a customer checks out the bundle and the merchant generates the label in WSS
- **Then** both the checkout-quoted rate and the WSS-booked label use 10 kg / 50×50×20 (parent only) — no child weights summed
- **And** the booked rate matches the rate shown at cart for the same shipping address

### Scenario: Bundled product with 'Sum Assembled Weight' continues to sum children (no regression)

- **Given** a bundle configured with Assembled Weight: Sum (parent 10 kg + 3 children each 1 kg)
- **When** rate/label is requested
- **Then** WSS uses 13 kg as the package weight (current correct behavior is preserved)
- **And** no other bundle configurations are silently changed by this fix

### Scenario: DIESELHEAT stops manual rate-correction work

- **Given** DIESELHEAT's ~150 labels/month over a 30-day window after fix release
- **When** the merchant generates labels for any bundled product order
- **Then** zero orders require post-quote manual dimension entry in WSS
- **And** the merchant can disable the workaround note in their internal SOP for fulfillment
