## User Story

As a **perfume retailer on Amazon Shipping who is actively losing inventory to package theft because product names on labels reveal high-value contents**,
I want **granular control over which product fields (name, SKU, quantity) appear on my shipping labels so I can obscure what is inside without losing tracking traceability**,
so that **I stop bleeding revenue to targeted theft, my warehouse team can still pick and verify orders by SKU, and I do not have to manually hack product descriptions every time I add a new item to my catalog**.

---

## Acceptance Criteria (Simple)

- [ ] Amazon Shipping labels print SKU and quantity in the item description field when the "show SKU/quantity" toggle is enabled for a store
- [ ] The existing "hide product description" toggle is split into granular controls: product name, SKU, and quantity can be independently shown or hidden
- [ ] Labels generated for store `948165-59.myshopify.com` (Account ID `6bab6a3b-6770-4fbd-8d0f-35cb81352953`) display SKU "Bestsellercombo" and quantity instead of blank or product name
- [ ] Multi-item orders display each line item's SKU and quantity correctly on the label
- [ ] The workaround (SKU stored in product description) is no longer required — native toggle produces correct output without manual product re-imports

---

## Acceptance Criteria (Given/When/Then)

### Scenario: Single-item order prints SKU and quantity natively
- **Given** a store has the "show SKU and quantity" toggle enabled for Amazon Shipping labels
- **When** a label is generated for order #2212 containing product "GSB Top 5 Best Seller Perfume Sample Combo Pack of 5" (SKU: Bestsellercombo, qty: 1)
- **Then** the item description field on the printed label reads "Bestsellercombo x 1" (or equivalent SKU + quantity format) and does NOT display the product name

### Scenario: Multi-item order prints all line items by SKU
- **Given** the toggle is enabled and an order contains 3 distinct products with different SKUs and quantities
- **When** a label is generated for that order
- **Then** each line item appears with its SKU and quantity, no product names are shown, and no line items are omitted

### Scenario: Toggle disabled preserves existing behavior
- **Given** a store has the "show SKU and quantity" toggle disabled (default)
- **When** a label is generated via Amazon Shipping
- **Then** the label displays the product name/description as it does today — no regression in default behavior
