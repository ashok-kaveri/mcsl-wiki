## Ticket Summary

# Ticket #374022 — Discounted Price Not Reflected on Commercial Invoice (BOGO)

- **Customer**: Hamza / Team Wing 999 (e20301.myshopify.com)
- **Duration**: 2026-02-13 to 2026-02-25 (ongoing)

## Timeline & Key Phases

### Phase 1: Problem Report (2026-02-13)
- Customer (Netherlands store) reported that UK customers are being charged import fees based on full product value even when Buy One Get One Free (BOGO) discount is applied.
- Example: customer paid 150 EUR for 2 items (one free) but was charged import fees on 300 EUR (full price for both).
- The Shopify order correctly shows 1 item at full price and 1 at $0, but the commercial invoice generated by the app uses the original (undiscounted) price.

### Phase 2: Investigation & Root Cause (2026-02-13 to 2026-02-19)
- Agent requested a sample order for testing.
- Customer had difficulty creating test orders via Shopify Bogus Gateway but shared a real BOGO order (#WING15975 and #WING15886).
- L3 confirmed root cause: discounted amounts are not being printed on the commercial invoice. (Comment #14)
- Fix committed for next release.

### Phase 3: Awaiting Fix (2026-02-19 to 2026-02-25)
- Customer acknowledged and asked to be notified when fix is implemented.
- Agent confirmed they will notify upon release.

## Open Issues
