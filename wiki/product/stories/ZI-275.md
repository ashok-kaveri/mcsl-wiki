## Acceptance Criteria (Given/When/Then)

### Scenario: Developer gets instant free trial account

- **Given** a developer partner applies for a free trial account
- **When** they complete the signup form with company info
- **Then** they receive account access within 2 hours
- **And** the account includes test/sandbox carrier integrations for development

### Scenario: Development store is pre-configured for testing

- **Given** a developer has been granted a free trial account
- **When** they log in
- **Then** the account has sample Shopify stores and test orders pre-loaded
- **And** they can immediately test label generation without setting up their own test store

### Scenario: Developer support is available

- **Given** a developer is testing the platform
- **When** they encounter integration questions
- **Then** they can access detailed API documentation
- **And** they can schedule a 30-minute technical consultation with the API team
