## User Story

As a **Shopify development agency building client stores that mix FedEx Express boxes (One Rate eligible) with custom packaging (not eligible), responsible for delivering a working checkout before client launch**,
I want **FedEx One Rate to appear at checkout only when the customer's cart fits in a FedEx Express box, and automatically hide when items require custom packaging**,
so that **my client's customers don't see a flat-rate shipping option they can't actually use, experience checkout friction when the rate disappears after selection, abandon their cart out of confusion, and I don't have to explain to my client why their professionally-built store has a broken shipping experience on day one**.

---

## Acceptance Criteria (Simple)

- [ ] App detects when order items fit in FedEx Express boxes vs require custom packaging
- [ ] FedEx One Rate displays at checkout only when all items fit in Express boxes
- [ ] When custom packaging is required, One Rate is hidden from rate selection (not grayed out/disabled)
- [ ] Rate calculation logic accounts for box packing rules when determining Express box eligibility
- [ ] Setting can be toggled per store: "Show FedEx One Rate only for Express boxes" (default: enabled)

---

## Acceptance Criteria (Given/When/Then)

### Scenario: Order fits in FedEx Express box, One Rate displays
- **Given** store has FedEx One Rate enabled and box packing configured
- **And** customer's cart contains items that fit in a FedEx Small Express box
- **When** customer views shipping rates at checkout
- **Then** FedEx One Rate appears as a selectable option with flat-rate price
- **And** customer can complete checkout with One Rate selected

### Scenario: Order requires custom packaging, One Rate hidden
- **Given** store has "Show One Rate only for Express boxes" enabled
- **And** customer's cart contains items requiring custom packaging (exceeds Express box dimensions)
- **When** customer views shipping rates at checkout
- **Then** FedEx One Rate does NOT appear in rate options
- **And** only Ground/other non-One-Rate FedEx services display

### Scenario: Mixed packaging scenario (some items fit, some don't)
- **Given** customer's cart has 2 items: one fits in Express Small, one requires custom box
- **When** box packing logic determines shipment needs 2 packages (1 Express + 1 custom)
- **Then** One Rate does NOT display (because at least one package uses custom packaging)
- **And** standard FedEx rates apply to entire order
