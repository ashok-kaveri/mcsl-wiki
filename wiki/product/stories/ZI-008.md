## Ticket Summary

# Ticket #306141 — FedEx Saturday Delivery Toggle & Day-of-Week Automation

- **Customer**: Samuel Rehal (BioPocrates / Zoguri)
- **Duration**: 2024-12-30 to 2025-04-10 (ongoing)

## Timeline & Key Phases

### Phase 1: Saturday Delivery Setup (2024-12-30 to 2025-01-07)
- Customer asked how to enable FedEx Saturday shipping for perishable items
- Support directed to Settings > Carriers > FedEx > Other Details > Enable "FedEx Saturday Service" (Comment #2)
- Customer enabled the setting but hit an error on non-Thursday/Friday days: "SATURDAY_DELIVERY is not allowed for the origin/destination pair" (Comment #3)
- Error occurred because Saturday Delivery is only valid when shipping on Thursday or Friday

### Phase 2: Root Cause & Workaround (2025-01-08)
- Senior team confirmed: SATURDAY_DELIVERY is only available on Thursday and Friday for enabled services (Comment #10)
- Workaround: customer must manually enable Saturday Delivery on Thursdays and disable it on other days
- Customer found this impractical and suggested a per-order checkbox for Saturday Delivery (Comment #12)
- Support acknowledged the suggestion and forwarded to development team (Comment #13)

### Phase 3: Follow-up & Confirmation (2025-04-10)
- Support followed up on the enhancement request (Comment #14)
- Customer confirmed still interested in automatic day-of-week handling and per-order toggle (Comment #15)
- Ticket moved back to dev_needed (Comment #17)

## Open Issues

1. **Automatic day-of-week Saturday Delivery handling** — App should automatically suppress SATURDAY_DELIVERY on Monday-Wednesday and only apply it on Thursday/Friday. Currently requires manual toggle. Blocked: dev team. Severity: moderate, affects perishable goods shipping workflow. Area: carrier-config. (Comment #17)
2. **Per-order Saturday Delivery toggle** — Customer requested a checkbox on individual orders to enable/disable Saturday Delivery instead of the global setting. Blocked: dev team (feature request). Severity: moderate. Area: feature-request. (Comment #12)

## Resolved Issues
