## User Story

As a **new Shopify store owner (Kamran) who just installed the shipping app**, I want **one-on-one onboarding support to set up carriers and complete my first shipping label**, so that **I feel confident using the app and don't abandon it due to setup uncertainty**.

## Acceptance Criteria (Given/When/Then)

### Scenario: New merchant gets assigned an onboarding support contact

- **Given** a new merchant installs the app
- **When** they request onboarding help
- **Then** they're assigned a specific support person who will guide them through setup
- **And** this person responds within 24 hours with personalized recommendations based on their shipping profile

### Scenario: First label is generated and shipped successfully

- **Given** a new merchant has completed onboarding
- **When** they generate a label for their first real order
- **Then** the label prints correctly and the carrier processes the shipment
- **And** they receive confirmation that shipment tracking is working

### Scenario: Onboarding reduces early churn

- **Given** onboarding support is provided
- **When** we track retention of supported vs unsupported new merchants
- **Then** merchants with onboarding support have 2x higher 30-day retention
- **And** they upgrade to paid plans at higher rates
