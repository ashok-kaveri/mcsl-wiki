## User Story

As a **customer retention manager**, I want **to programmatically identify merchants who uninstall the app and send them targeted win-back campaigns with incentives**, so that **we reduce churn and recover merchants who abandoned the app due to minor issues**.

---

## Acceptance Criteria (Given/When/Then)

### Scenario: Uninstall detection triggers immediate win-back

- **Given** any Shopify merchant uninstalls the app
- **When** the webhook is received
- **Then** a win-back email is sent within 30 minutes
- **And** the email offers a specific incentive (extended trial or % discount)

### Scenario: Win-back email is personalized

- **Given** a merchant uninstalled
- **When** they receive the win-back email
- **Then** the email references their store name and previous usage (e.g., "We noticed you used USPS labels")
- **And** the email offers a solution to the likely problem (e.g., "Carrier setup giving you trouble? We offer 1-on-1 setup support")

### Scenario: Win-back ROI is measured

- **Given** win-back campaigns run
- **When** we measure effectiveness
- **Then** we track email open rate, click-through rate, and reinstall conversion
- **And** we iterate on messaging based on what drives reinstalls
