## Timeline & Key Phases

### Phase 1: Rate log visibility issue (2026-04-27 18:11 - 18:59)
- Customer unable to view shipping quote details in rate request logs (Comment #1)
- Checkout rate failure notification displayed
- Issue resolved on its own - logs became visible after initial report (Comment #5)

### Phase 2: FedEx rate discrepancy investigation (2026-04-27 18:59 - 21:32)
- Customer identified significant rate discrepancy after reviewing logs (Comment #5)
  - App quoted: $71.24
  - Customer charged (after 10% padding): $78.36
  - Actual FedEx cost: $94.96
  - **Loss to merchant: $23.72 per shipment**
- Customer identified root cause: FedEx sub-package type set to "Skid" instead of "Box" (Comment #5)
- Multi-package shipment quoted incorrectly as single skid
- Support changed FedEx Other Details sub-package type from Skid to Box (Comment #7)
- Customer tested after change - rate still inaccurate, FedEx.com confirmed $94.96 (Comment #8)
- Support requested comparison details to identify mismatch (address type, pickup type, special services) (Comment #9)

### Phase 3: Call scheduled for deeper investigation (2026-04-28 07:37 - ongoing)
- Customer scheduled support call for April 28, 2026 at 09:45 am ET (Comment #10, #11)
- Meeting link to be shared 10-15 min before scheduled time (Comment #12)

## Open Issues

1. **FedEx rate quotes significantly lower than actual shipping costs** — App quoting $71.24 for shipments that cost $94.96 at FedEx (33% underquote), causing $23.72 loss per shipment. Changing sub-package type from Skid to Box did not resolve discrepancy. Likely mismatch in package details, residential/commercial address classification, pickup type, or special services between app request and actual FedEx requirements. Requires detailed comparison of app vs FedEx.com shipment parameters. Blocked: customer (losing money on every affected shipment). Severity: P2 (direct revenue loss on shipments). Area: rate-shopping. (Comment #5, #8, #9)

## Resolved Issues

1. **Rate log visibility failure** — Temporary issue where rate request logs weren't displaying in app. Resolved on its own without intervention. (Comment #1, #5)

2. **FedEx sub-package type misconfiguration** — Changed from "Skid" to "Box" under App → Settings → Carrier → FedEx → Other Details. However, this configuration change did not resolve the underlying rate discrepancy. (Comment #7)

As a **merchant who ships 623 orders over 53 days and relies on accurate rate quotes to price shipping for customers**,
