## User Story

As a **Shopify store manager who regularly removes discontinued products from my catalog, and whose StorePep product list still shows items I deleted months ago in Shopify**,
I want **product deletions in Shopify to automatically propagate to StorePep so my product list stays in sync without manual cleanup**,
so that **my warehouse team never picks a discontinued product for shipping, I stop wasting time manually removing stale entries, and the product count in StorePep actually matches what's live in my store**.

---

## Acceptance Criteria (Simple)

- [ ] Deleting a product in Shopify removes it from StorePep product list within the next sync cycle (no manual intervention)
- [ ] Bulk deletions (e.g., 50+ seasonal products removed at once) propagate completely — no orphaned entries remain
- [ ] Deleted products no longer appear in product search, label generation dropdowns, or automation rule product filters
- [ ] Sync log records each deletion with timestamp for audit trail
- [ ] David B.'s store shows zero stale/deleted products after the fix ships

---

## Acceptance Criteria (Given/When/Then)

### Scenario: Single product deletion syncs
- **Given** "Widget A" exists in both Shopify and StorePep
- **When** the merchant deletes "Widget A" from Shopify
- **Then** "Widget A" is removed from StorePep on the next sync cycle
- **And** the sync log records "Deleted: Widget A (SKU: WDG-A) — synced from Shopify deletion"

### Scenario: Bulk seasonal cleanup
- **Given** 40 seasonal holiday products exist in StorePep, all deleted from Shopify after Christmas
- **When** the next sync cycle runs
- **Then** all 40 products are removed from StorePep
- **And** no orphaned variants or product fragments remain searchable

### Scenario: Deleted product blocked from label generation
- **Given** "Widget A" was deleted from Shopify but an old order references it
- **When** a label is generated for that order
- **Then** the label uses the product data from the order record (not the deleted product entry)
- **And** "Widget A" does not reappear in the StorePep product list
