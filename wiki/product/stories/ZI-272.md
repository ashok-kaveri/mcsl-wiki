## User Story

As a **shipping logistics coordinator who tried the app but uninstalled**, I want **to be offered a fresh opportunity with an extended free trial and guidance on how to use the features correctly**, so that **I can give the app another chance without commitment while getting the support I needed the first time**.

---

## Acceptance Criteria (Given/When/Then)

### Scenario: Win-back offer includes extended trial + support

- **Given** a merchant uninstalled the app
- **When** they receive the win-back email
- **Then** the offer includes: 30-day extended free trial + 1-hour onboarding consultation
- **And** they can claim both benefits by clicking a single link (no additional email exchange)

### Scenario: Reinstall experience is smooth

- **Given** a merchant clicks the win-back offer link
- **When** they reinstall the app
- **Then** they're immediately logged in with their previous store configuration
- **And** a calendar link to book the onboarding consultation is prominently displayed

### Scenario: Win-back success is tracked

- **Given** win-back campaigns are launched
- **When** we measure results
- **Then** we track % of recipients who click, reinstall, and claim the consultation
- **And** we compare win-back success by messaging variant
