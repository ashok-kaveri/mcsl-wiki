# Ticket #369144 — Label Generation Failing Due to Invalid Customs Value

- **Customer**: Ella Beattie (satisfyrunning.myshopify.com)
- **Duration**: 2026-01-15 to 2026-02-09 (ongoing — dev_needed tag remains)

## Timeline & Key Phases

### Phase 1: Report & Investigation (2026-01-15)
- Customer reported label generation failures for 4 orders (#112295, #112261, #112220, #112259) — all merged into this ticket.
- All failing orders contained the same product ("Possessed Magazine - One Size") with customs value of 1 EUR.
- Currency conversion issue: 1 EUR to USD resulted in a value below the minimum customs threshold, triggering "Invalid Customs Value" error.

### Phase 2: Workaround & Fix (2026-01-15 to 2026-01-16)
- Agent advised customer to update customs value; customer confirmed update.
- Labels successfully generated for order #112295 after resync; customer asked to generate remaining labels.
- L3 noted an enhancement to pass minimum customs value as 1 (country-wise pricing update) — targeted for end of January / first week of February.
