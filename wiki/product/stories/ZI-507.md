## Acceptance Criteria (Given/When/Then)

### Scenario: Customer generates a WorldEase movement end-to-end

- **Given** a UPS-enabled France store with WorldEase entitlement and 8 international orders queued
- **When** the merchant selects WorldEase and generates labels in a single batch
- **Then** the app creates one master shipment with GCCN, attaches 8 child shipments, and successfully closes the movement within 7 days
- **And** all 8 child labels reference the correct GCCN

### Scenario: Delete-child-shipment works mid-movement

- **Given** an open WorldEase movement with 5 child shipments
- **When** the merchant cancels one of the 5 orders and triggers a child-delete in WSS
- **Then** the delete-child API call to UPS succeeds and the movement reduces to 4 children
- **And** the close-out then succeeds on the remaining 4

### Scenario: POE table is shared with the customer during onboarding

- **Given** a new merchant enabling WorldEase for the first time
- **When** they complete the onboarding step
- **Then** the documentation provides the POE table reference that UPS requires and explains how to share it
- **And** no out-of-band email or support follow-up is required to activate WorldEase
