## User Story

As a **PluginHive partner providing white-label shipping to resellers**,
I want **clear, proactive support and structured onboarding workflows that anticipate my next steps**,
so that **I spend less time waiting for responses and can move from trial to production faster, reducing my time-to-value and friction in the buying process**.

---

## Acceptance Criteria

### Scenario: New merchant receives structured onboarding sequence
- **Given** a merchant signs up and completes initial configuration
- **When** they submit their first 5 test orders
- **Then** they automatically receive proactive check-in at day 2, day 7, and day 30 with next-step guidance
- **And** each check-in includes specific actions ("ready to go live?", "need carrier setup?", "scaling questions?")

### Scenario: Partner reseller can configure customer-specific rates
- **Given** a PluginHive partner manages shipping for 10+ reseller stores
- **When** they need to apply different carrier negotiated rates per customer
- **Then** the UI supports rate templates or per-customer carrier configuration without requiring support tickets
- **And** rate changes apply immediately to new labels without manual API calls

### Scenario: Prevent onboarding drop-off with self-serve resources
- **Given** a merchant is stuck on UPS account setup or carrier configuration
- **When** they haven't heard from support in 24 hours
- **Then** the app displays contextual help, video tutorials, or knowledge base links (not just "contact support")
- **And** we track which merchants are stuck (to identify common blockers and proactively improve the flow)
