## User Story

As a **merchant who tried the app but uninstalled**, I want **an immediate, frictionless way to re-engage with the app with an extended trial and clear next steps**, so that **I don't have to go through installation and setup again and can quickly resume shipping if I've resolved the issue that caused the uninstall**.

---

## Acceptance Criteria (Given/When/Then)

### Scenario: Win-back email converts uninstalled merchant back

- **Given** a merchant uninstalled the app
- **When** they receive the win-back offer (30-day trial extension + support)
- **Then** 20%+ of recipients click the win-back link
- **And** 50%+ of clickers reinstall the app within 7 days

### Scenario: Reinstalled app has zero additional setup friction

- **Given** a merchant clicks the win-back link
- **When** they reinstall and log in
- **Then** all their previous carrier configurations are restored
- **And** they can generate a label within 30 seconds (no reconfiguration needed)

### Scenario: Support is available immediately after reinstall

- **Given** a reinstalled merchant
- **When** they access the app
- **Then** a support chat option is prominently visible
- **And** they can schedule the included onboarding consultation in the app itself
