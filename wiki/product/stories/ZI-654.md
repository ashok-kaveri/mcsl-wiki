## User Story

As a **Shopify merchant who ships internationally and routinely edits shipping charges after order placement to reflect custom freight quotes**,
I want **the commercial invoice freight cost to reflect only the current active shipping charge, not the sum of all historical edits stored by Shopify**,
so that **my customs declarations show accurate freight values and I don't risk compliance issues or have to abandon the app and generate labels directly on the FedEx website**.

---

## Acceptance Criteria (Simple)

- [ ] When reading Shopify's `total_shipping_price_set`, the app extracts only the current active shipping line amount, ignoring deleted/superseded historical entries
- [ ] Commercial invoice freight charges match the shipping amount displayed in Shopify's order admin UI after edits
- [ ] Orders with multiple shipping edits (delete + re-add cycles) produce correct freight values on the commercial invoice
- [ ] The MCSL dashboard order total also reflects the corrected shipping amount after auto-sync or re-import
- [ ] Existing orders that were never edited continue to show correct freight charges (regression guard)

---

## Acceptance Criteria (Given/When/Then)

### Scenario: Edited shipping charge reflected correctly on commercial invoice
- **Given** a Shopify order originally had a shipping charge of EUR 530.79, which the merchant deleted and replaced with EUR 508 via Shopify's Edit Order flow
- **When** the merchant generates a FedEx international label with commercial invoice in MCSL
- **Then** the commercial invoice shows EUR 508 as the freight charge, not EUR 1,038.79 (the sum of both entries)

### Scenario: Multiple shipping edits produce correct single value
- **Given** a Shopify order has had its shipping charge edited three times (EUR 530.79, then EUR 479, then EUR 508) with each previous entry deleted before adding the new one
- **When** the app reads the order data via Shopify API and calculates the freight cost for the commercial invoice
- **Then** only the final active shipping entry (EUR 508) is used, and the invoice freight charge equals EUR 508

### Scenario: Unedited orders remain unaffected
- **Given** a Shopify order was placed with a single shipping charge of EUR 50 and the merchant never edited the shipping cost
- **When** the merchant generates an international label with commercial invoice in MCSL
- **Then** the commercial invoice shows EUR 50 as the freight charge, confirming the fix does not alter behavior for unedited orders
