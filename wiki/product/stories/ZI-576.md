## User Story

As a **Shopify merchant paying for two FedEx apps simultaneously — MCSL and the legacy "Ship Rate & Track" app — just to show customers the cheapest Saturday shipping option, because MCSL bundles Saturday Delivery as a surcharge on Priority Overnight instead of showing it as a separate service with its own rate comparison**,
I want **MCSL to display FedEx Priority Overnight and Priority Overnight Saturday Delivery as two separate checkout services, each showing the cheaper of One Rate vs account-negotiated pricing**,
so that **I can retire the legacy app, stop paying double, and let my 500+ monthly orders show customers the actual cheapest Saturday option without me manually comparing rates across two dashboards**.

## Acceptance Criteria (Given/When/Then)

### Scenario: Saturday Delivery as separate checkout service
- **Given** a Shopify store with FedEx account rates and One Rate both enabled, customer checking out on Saturday-eligible day
- **When** checkout fetches rates from MCSL
- **Then** "FedEx Priority Overnight" and "FedEx Priority Overnight Saturday Delivery" appear as separate line items
- **And** the Saturday option shows the cheaper of One Rate vs account rate

### Scenario: Rate comparison picks cheapest Saturday option
- **Given** One Rate Saturday = $45 and account Saturday rate = $38
- **When** MCSL calculates Saturday delivery rates
- **Then** checkout displays $38 (account rate) for Saturday Delivery
- **And** rate source is logged for support diagnostics

### Scenario: Non-Saturday orders unaffected
- **Given** a customer checking out on a weekday (no Saturday eligibility)
- **When** checkout fetches rates
- **Then** only standard Priority Overnight appears (no Saturday variant)
- **And** existing rate comparison behavior unchanged
