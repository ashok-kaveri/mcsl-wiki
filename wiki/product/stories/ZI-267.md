## User Story

As a **new Shopify merchant just installed the shipping app**, I want **a guided, step-by-step onboarding flow that sets up my carriers and generates my first label without confusion**, so that **I experience immediate success and can start shipping orders within minutes instead of abandoning the app because setup seems complicated**.

---

## Acceptance Criteria (Given/When/Then)

### Scenario: New merchant completes onboarding within 5 minutes

- **Given** I've just installed the app and opened it for the first time
- **When** I click "Get Started"
- **Then** the system shows a 3-step workflow: (1) Select carriers, (2) Add carrier credentials, (3) Generate test label
- **And** each step has clear instructions and examples

### Scenario: First label generation succeeds with onboarding data

- **Given** I've completed the onboarding workflow
- **When** I attempt to generate a label for a real order
- **Then** the label generates successfully on the first try
- **And** the carrier account is validated before I try to generate

### Scenario: New merchants don't churn due to setup friction

- **Given** any new merchant
- **When** they complete onboarding
- **Then** they either generate a label or receive support help within 24 hours
- **And** we don't have high churn in week 1 due to setup complexity
