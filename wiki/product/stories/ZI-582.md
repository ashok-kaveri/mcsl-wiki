## User Story

As a **$49 Premium Shopify merchant in Perth shipping 900-1,600 bulky lawn equipment parcels per month to regional Australian towns, who brought TGE and PluginHive together four months ago and whose carrier partner has already delivered API docs, sandbox credentials, and test ranges**,
I want **Team Global Express integrated into MCSL with both Road Express and IPEC Priority services so I can rate-shop TGE alongside my existing Australia Post and Aramex accounts from a single dashboard**,
so that **I stop bleeding 10-15% more per shipment on carriers whose regional town pricing cannot compete with my negotiated TGE contract rates, and I finally get supply chain resilience instead of being locked into two carriers for a business that ships thousands of parcels monthly**.

## Acceptance Criteria (Simple)

- [ ] TGE OAuth authentication succeeds using the provided UAT client credentials and the carrier appears in MCSL carrier settings
- [ ] Rate shopping returns TGE Road Express and IPEC Priority quotes for Australian domestic routes with contract pricing reflected
- [ ] Label generation produces valid TGE shipping labels with tracking numbers, booking confirmation, and correct SHIPMENT ID/SSCC ranges
- [ ] Tracking status syncs from TGE API into MCSL order timeline and Shopify order page within 5 minutes of carrier scan
- [ ] Mighty Goat Lawns (billy-goat-lawn-care.myshopify.com) completes end-to-end UAT with 5+ test labels across both Road Express and IPEC services before production release
- [ ] Manifest/end-of-day close-out API call is implemented so TGE can schedule pickups from generated manifests
- [ ] Cube-factor 250 dimensional weight calculation is supported for bulky goods routing

## Acceptance Criteria (Given/When/Then)

### Scenario: TGE carrier onboarding via OAuth
- **Given** a merchant has TGE API credentials (client ID, client secret) and the MCSL carrier settings page is open
- **When** they enter their TGE credentials and click connect
- **Then** OAuth handshake completes, TGE appears as an active carrier with Road Express and IPEC Priority services listed, and a test API call confirms connectivity

### Scenario: Australian domestic label generation with TGE Road Express
- **Given** an order shipping from Perth, WA to Sydney, NSW via TGE Road Express with a 5kg parcel (30x20x15cm)
- **When** the merchant generates a shipping label
- **Then** a valid TGE label PDF is returned with a tracking number, booking confirmation, and correct SHIPMENT ID from the allocated range, and tracking syncs to the Shopify order timeline

### Scenario: Rate comparison across multiple AU carriers including TGE
- **Given** TGE, Australia Post, and Aramex MyFastway are all enabled for the store
- **When** rate shopping runs for Perth to Melbourne (8kg bulky item, 60x40x30cm, cube-factor 250)
- **Then** all three carriers return quotes with transit time estimates, TGE rates reflect the merchant's negotiated contract pricing, and dimensional weight is calculated using TGE's cube-factor 250 formula
