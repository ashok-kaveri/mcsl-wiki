## User Story

As a **WooCommerce merchant who didn't choose their hosting security stack**,
I want **my orders to import reliably regardless of what firewall or CDN sits in front of my store**,
so that **my fulfillment pipeline isn't blocked for a month by infrastructure I don't control, and I don't need to become a Cloudflare expert just to ship my products**.

---

## Acceptance Criteria (Simple)

- [ ] Order sync works for stores behind Cloudflare, Sucuri, and other common WAFs — not just on unprotected hosts
- [ ] When sync is blocked by a WAF, the app detects it (doesn't silently fail) and tells the merchant exactly what to whitelist
- [ ] A self-serve Cloudflare/WAF setup guide exists so support doesn't spend 42 comments on one ticket
- [ ] This specific customer (secondshotmarket.com) is unblocked and syncing
- [ ] Webhook and manual "Sync Now" both work through WAF-protected stores

---

## Acceptance Criteria (Detailed — Given/When/Then)

### Scenario 1: Sync detects WAF blocking and gives actionable guidance
- **Given** a WooCommerce store has Cloudflare (or similar WAF) returning a challenge page instead of API JSON
- **When** the app attempts to sync orders and receives an HTML response instead of JSON
- **Then** the app recognizes this as a WAF block (not a generic "sync failed")
- **And** displays: "Your store's firewall is blocking our requests. Whitelist these IPs: [list]. See our [WAF setup guide]."

### Scenario 2: Orders flow after whitelisting
- **Given** the merchant has whitelisted PluginHive IPs in their Cloudflare dashboard
- **When** a new order is placed on the store
- **Then** the webhook delivers the order to PluginHive within 60 seconds
- **And** manual "Sync Now" also returns orders successfully

### Scenario 3: App handles intermittent WAF challenges gracefully
- **Given** a WAF occasionally challenges requests (rate limiting, bot score fluctuation)
- **When** the app gets a challenge on one sync attempt but not the next
- **Then** the app retries with backoff instead of marking the sync as permanently failed
- **And** orders that were missed during challenge windows are picked up on the next successful sync
