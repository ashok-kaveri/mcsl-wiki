# Ticket #395207 — FedEx Brazil Label Fails Due to Incorrect tinType for Recipient CNPJ/CPF

- **Customer**: Anna Jo (satisfyrunning.myshopify.com)
- **Product**: Shopify Multi Carrier Shipping Label
- **Duration**: 2026-06-15 to 2026-06-18 (4 days, ongoing)

## Timeline & Key Phases

### Phase 1: Customer Reports Recurring CNPJ/CPF Label Error (2026-06-15)
- Customer Anna Jo reports Order #136281 failing with "The tax ID number and/or CNPJ/CPF is invalid" — same error as a prior occurrence
- Customer has confirmed CPF `025.474.405-20` is correct; previously resolved by generating label manually on FedEx website
- Order involves multiple items with detailed package contents required for international shipping
- Support escalates to L3 after acknowledging the issue

### Phase 2: Technical Team Confirms App Data is Correct, Escalates to FedEx (2026-06-16)
- Engineering (Veena) reviews and confirms all data including CPF is being passed correctly from the app
- Support team calls FedEx helpdesk (+1 877-339-2774, 15 min 49 sec call, Case ID TR4188034)
- FedEx support identifies root cause: the `tinType` field for recipient is being passed as `BUSINESS_NATIONAL` — FedEx suggests it should be one of `PERSONAL_NATIONAL`, `PERSONAL_STATE`, `FEDERAL`, `BUSINESS_NATIONAL`, `BUSINESS_STATE`, `BUSINESS_UNION`
- Customer informed that fix is in progress; manual FedEx website workaround advised in the interim

### Phase 3: App Enhancement Scoped and ETA Set (2026-06-17–2026-06-18)
- Engineering (Veena) confirms app will be enhanced to pass appropriate `tinType` for recipient tax ID
- Ticket tagged `dev_needed`; ETA set for on or before 7 July 2026
- Customer notified that enhancement is in progress

## Open Issues

1. **App Hardcodes BUSINESS_NATIONAL tinType for FedEx International Recipient Tax IDs** — The app always sends `tinType: BUSINESS_NATIONAL` for recipient CNPJ/CPF on FedEx international shipments. For Brazilian recipients with personal CPF numbers, this causes FedEx to reject the label request. The app needs to expose or infer the correct `tinType` value based on the recipient tax ID type. Engineering has scoped the enhancement but it is not yet shipped. Blocked: customer (cannot generate label via app), engineering (implementation pending). Severity: recurring failure on international FedEx shipments to Brazil; customer has had to resort to manual workaround on both occurrences; ETA 7 July. Area: label-generation. (Comment #13, #14, #17)

## Resolved Issues

1. **Identified Root Cause of CNPJ/CPF Rejection** — FedEx confirmed via case TR4188034 that the rejection is caused by incorrect `tinType` being sent, not invalid tax ID data. App data confirmed correct. (Comment #8, #13)

## User Story

As a **high-volume international merchant on a $200 Custom plan shipping from France to Brazil via FedEx**,
I want **the app to send the correct tax identification type when my Brazilian customers provide personal CPF numbers versus business CNPJ numbers**,
so that **I stop losing hours on every Brazil-bound order manually generating labels on the FedEx website while my customers wait for their shipments**.

---

## Acceptance Criteria (Simple)

- [ ] When a FedEx international shipment includes a recipient CPF (11-digit Brazilian personal tax ID), the app sends `tinType: PERSONAL_NATIONAL` (not `BUSINESS_NATIONAL`)
- [ ] When a FedEx international shipment includes a recipient CNPJ (14-digit Brazilian business tax ID), the app sends `tinType: BUSINESS_NATIONAL`
- [ ] Anna Jo (satisfyrunning.myshopify.com) can generate a FedEx label for Order #136281 without resorting to the FedEx website
- [ ] The tinType mapping logic is driven by configuration (not hardcoded) so additional country-specific tax ID types can be added without code changes
- [ ] Existing FedEx international shipments to non-Brazil destinations with no recipient tax ID continue to work without regression
