## User Story

As a **new owner (David Fowler) who just took over an active Shopify merchant (Healthie Wholesalers) shipping 239 labels/month on a $29 plan via AuPost MPB + Aramex**,
I want **to self-serve the change of primary contact and co-authorized user from the app's billing/profile page without emailing support**,
so that **I can close out the ownership handover cleanly during the 4-6 week transition, both Tamara and I start receiving renewal/billing notices, and I don't have to chase PluginHive during a period when the old owner is still on the thread**.

## Acceptance Criteria (Simple)

- [ ] Primary contact for account `Healthie Wholesalers / TND Ventures Pty Ltd` is switched to David Fowler with Tamara South added as an authorized user.

## Acceptance Criteria (Given/When/Then)

### Scenario: Apply this specific ownership change
- **Given** Healthie Wholesalers account has David Fowler's confirmation in Comment #5 (primary = David, co-owner = Tamara, emails unchanged)
- **When** PluginHive applies the contact update
- **Then** the account's primary contact is David Fowler and Tamara is listed as authorized
- **And** the next billing event's notification goes to David's inbox, not Trent's

### Scenario: Self-serve change-of-ownership for any merchant
- **Given** an existing merchant on any plan initiates a change of ownership
- **When** they submit a change-of-ownership form from the app (new primary name/phone, optional new email, optional co-authorized user)
- **Then** the change is applied without manual support intervention
- **And** an audit trail entry is written with old and new contact values plus the initiating user

### Scenario: Prevent orphaned renewals during ownership handover
- **Given** a plan renews during an ownership handover window
- **When** the renewal email is dispatched
- **Then** it goes to the current primary contact on record
- **And** billing history remains attached to the same account ID so the new owner sees past invoices
