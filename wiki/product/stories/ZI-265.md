## User Story

As a **high-volume Shopify merchant paying $49/month who's shipping 900+ orders per two weeks**, I want **instant access to a 6-month historical report showing shipping volume, cost trends, and carrier performance**, so that **I can analyze which carriers are performing best and negotiate better rates with my top carriers without waiting for L3 support to compile data manually**.

---

## Acceptance Criteria (Given/When/Then)

### Scenario: Merchant generates 6-month report on-demand

- **Given** I'm logged into the app with an active subscription
- **When** I navigate to Reports and select "Historical Shipping Summary"
- **Then** the system generates a 6-month report in under 10 seconds
- **And** the report shows volume by carrier, cost per carrier, and delivery performance metrics

### Scenario: Report includes actionable insights for high-volume stores

- **Given** my store has shipped 5000+ orders in the past 6 months
- **When** I view the report
- **Then** it highlights my top 3 carriers by volume
- **And** it shows cost-per-shipment trends so I can spot if one carrier's rates increased

### Scenario: Report-generation doesn't impact daily operations

- **Given** I'm generating a historical report
- **When** the report is being compiled
- **Then** my ability to generate labels continues uninterrupted
- **And** the report doesn't timeout even for stores with 10k+ historical orders
