## User Story

As a **merchant who negotiated a custom yearly plan with sales based on my actual shipping volume and budget constraints**,
I want **the app billing infrastructure to support annual subscription cycles with defined shipment quotas**,
so that **I can commit to a full year contract that matches my seasonal shipping patterns (100-300/month off-season, 1000-1500/month during peak Diwali season) without being forced into monthly plans that don't reflect my actual usage, and the sales team can close deals with strategic partners like Amazon India without being blocked by platform limitations**.

## Acceptance Criteria (Simple)

- [ ] MCSL app can accept yearly billing cycles ($150/year, $200/year, etc.)
- [ ] Yearly plans can specify shipment quotas (e.g., 4000 labels/year, 8000 labels/year)
- [ ] Yearly plans can specify carrier limits (e.g., 2 carriers, 5 carriers)
- [ ] Customer silviya-co.myshopify.com can be provisioned with $150/year plan for 4000 shipments + 2 carriers + tracking
- [ ] Payment collection mechanism exists for yearly plans (direct payment link or Shopify billing API)
- [ ] Yearly plan customers can see remaining quota and renewal date in app dashboard
- [ ] Sales team can create custom yearly plans without waiting for dev work

## Acceptance Criteria (Given/When/Then)

### Scenario: Amazon-referred customer converts on negotiated yearly plan
- **Given** customer silviya-co.myshopify.com has agreed to $150/year plan for 4000 shipments + 2 carriers
- **When** sales team provisions the yearly plan via admin dashboard or direct payment link
- **Then** customer receives payment link, pays $150, and plan activates for 365 days with 4000 shipment quota
- **And** customer can generate labels using Amazon Shipping and BlueDart (2 carriers)
- **And** customer can see "3847 shipments remaining" and "Renews 2027-04-01" in app dashboard

### Scenario: Broader yearly plan support for any pricing tier
- **Given** a customer wants a yearly plan at any tier (e.g., $200/year for 8000 shipments)
- **When** sales team configures the yearly plan parameters (price, shipment quota, carrier limit, duration)
- **Then** the plan is available in the app, billing occurs annually, and quota resets on renewal
- **And** monthly customers can switch to equivalent yearly plans mid-cycle (prorated credit)

### Scenario: Self-serve yearly plan selection prevents future escalations
- **Given** a new customer evaluates the app during trial
- **When** they view subscription plan options in-app
- **Then** they can choose between monthly OR yearly billing for each tier (Starter, Growth, Pro)
- **And** yearly plans show total cost, per-month equivalent, shipment quota, and savings vs monthly
- **And** no support ticket is needed to access yearly billing — it's a standard option
