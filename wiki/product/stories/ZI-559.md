## User Story

As a **PluginHive onboarding lead bringing a second high-volume Indian Ayurvedic merchant (Aswini) onto the same operational profile that Loveofindia already runs on**, I want **a single named "LOI profile" bundle of feature flags that I can apply to a new MCSL store in one step — instead of hand-listing six to ten individual toggles in a Slack message and hoping the platform team enables every one before I move the ticket to on-hold**, so that **toggle drift between LOI and Aswini does not surface a month from now as a "why does LOI sort labels by product count but our store doesn't" complaint, and the third merchant we onboard onto this profile takes minutes rather than days.**
