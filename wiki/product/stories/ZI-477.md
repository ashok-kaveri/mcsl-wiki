## User Story

As a **BigCommerce merchant shipping internationally with PostNord who is legally required to declare accurate customs values**,
I want **customs duties calculated on the grand total (subtotal + shipping + fees) not just the product subtotal**,
so that **I comply with customs regulations, avoid potential fines for incorrect declarations, and my customers aren't surprised by unexpected duty charges when the receiving country uses the correct calculation**.
