## User Story

As a **high-volume Indian e-commerce merchant shipping 4,500 parcels per month from Chennai through multiple domestic carriers**,
I want **Professional Couriers available as an integrated carrier option in the StorePep app alongside BlueDart, IndiaPost, and DTDC**,
so that **I can compare rates across all four carriers, generate labels, and manage shipments from a single dashboard without logging into separate carrier portals for each shipment**.

---

## Acceptance Criteria (Simple)

- [ ] Professional Couriers appears as an available carrier in the carrier registration flow with credential input fields matching their API requirements
- [ ] After successful registration, the merchant can fetch live shipping rates from Professional Couriers for domestic Indian shipments
- [ ] Label generation produces a valid Professional Couriers shipping label with correct barcode, sender/receiver details, and service type
- [ ] Shipment tracking status from Professional Couriers API is displayed on the order detail page alongside other carrier tracking data
- [ ] Rate comparison on the order summary page includes Professional Couriers rates alongside BlueDart, IndiaPost, and DTDC when all four carriers are configured

---

## Acceptance Criteria (Given/When/Then)

### Scenario: Carrier registration with Professional Couriers credentials
- **Given** a merchant has obtained API credentials from Professional Couriers (API key, customer code, and sandbox/production endpoint)
- **When** they enter these credentials in the StorePep carrier registration page and submit
- **Then** the app validates the credentials against Professional Couriers' API and shows a successful connection confirmation

### Scenario: Rate fetching for domestic Indian shipment
- **Given** a merchant has Professional Couriers registered and an order with origin Chennai and destination Mumbai weighing 2.5 kg
- **When** the merchant opens the order summary page and requests shipping rates
- **Then** Professional Couriers returns at least one service option with rate, estimated delivery time, and service name displayed alongside other configured carriers

### Scenario: Label generation and tracking for Professional Couriers shipment
- **Given** a merchant has selected Professional Couriers as the carrier for an order and confirmed the shipment details
- **When** they click Generate Label on the order
- **Then** a shipping label PDF is generated with correct Professional Couriers barcode, consignment number, and the shipment status becomes trackable via the app's tracking page
