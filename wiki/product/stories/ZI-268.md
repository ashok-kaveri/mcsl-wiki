## User Story

As a **customer support team member managing churn**, I want **to automatically detect uninstalls and send personalized win-back offers with trial extensions**, so that **we can recover merchants who encountered minor friction instead of losing them permanently, improving our retention rate and LTV**.

---

## Acceptance Criteria (Given/When/Then)

### Scenario: Uninstall triggers win-back workflow

- **Given** a merchant uninstalls the app
- **When** the webhook notifies our system
- **Then** a win-back email is sent within 1 hour offering a 30-day extended trial
- **And** the email mentions specific features they might have missed (carrier comparison, rate optimization)

### Scenario: Win-back offer can be redeemed self-serve

- **Given** a merchant receives the win-back email
- **When** they click "Claim extended trial"
- **Then** their account is reinstated with 30 additional trial days
- **And** they don't have to email support to take advantage of the offer

### Scenario: Win-back success is measured

- **Given** win-back campaigns are run
- **When** we track redemption rates
- **Then** we measure % of uninstalled merchants who reinstall
- **And** we identify which messaging/incentives drive highest win-back rates
