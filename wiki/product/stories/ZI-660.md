## User Story

As a **small New Zealand-based Shopify merchant shipping fewer than NZD 10,000 worth of parcels annually and ineligible for a deferred-payment NZ Post credit account**,
I want **to connect my MyNZPB (My NZ Post for Business) pay-as-you-go account to the StorePep app**,
so that **I can access NZ Post domestic and international shipping rates, generate labels, and manage shipments directly from StorePep without needing to qualify for a full commercial NZ Post account**.

---

## Acceptance Criteria (Simple)

- [ ] MyNZPB appears as a separate account type option in the NZ Post carrier registration flow, distinct from the existing NZ Post credit/deferred-payment account
- [ ] Merchants can authenticate with their MyNZPB credentials (OAuth or API key) and the app validates the connection against the MyNZPB API
- [ ] Authenticated MyNZPB accounts can fetch live domestic and international shipping rates from NZ Post
- [ ] Label generation through a MyNZPB account produces valid NZ Post shipping labels with correct barcodes, tracking numbers, and consignment details
- [ ] The MyNZPB integration follows the same registration UI pattern established by the Australia Post MPB integration for consistency

---

## Acceptance Criteria (Given/When/Then)

### Scenario: MyNZPB account registration and validation
- **Given** a New Zealand merchant has a MyNZPB (My NZ Post for Business) account with pay-as-you-go billing and valid API credentials
- **When** they select "MyNZPB" as the account type in the NZ Post carrier registration page and enter their credentials
- **Then** the app connects to the MyNZPB API, validates the credentials, and displays the account as successfully registered with the merchant's business name

### Scenario: Rate fetching for MyNZPB domestic shipment
- **Given** a merchant has a registered MyNZPB account and an order shipping from Auckland to Wellington weighing 1.5 kg
- **When** they open the order summary page and request shipping rates
- **Then** the app returns NZ Post domestic service options (including ParcelPost, CourierPost, and Express where eligible) with pay-as-you-go pricing from the MyNZPB rate card

### Scenario: Label generation and tracking via MyNZPB account
- **Given** a merchant has selected a NZ Post service via their MyNZPB account for an order and confirmed the shipment details
- **When** they click Generate Label
- **Then** a valid NZ Post shipping label is generated with a trackable consignment number, the shipment is billable to the MyNZPB pay-as-you-go account, and tracking updates appear on the order tracking page
