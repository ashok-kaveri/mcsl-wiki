## User Story

As a **cross-border Shopify merchant based in Montreal who ships inventory from China through Lebanon to North America, paying $19/month for MCSL but unable to use my preferred China-origin carrier YunExpress because it's not integrated — while sandbox credentials and API docs have been sitting with the dev team for over 2 weeks with no feasibility assessment started**,
I want **YunExpress available as a carrier in MCSL with credential setup, rate shopping, label generation, and tracking — plus UPS Importer of Record configuration for third-party duty billing on cross-border routes**,
so that **I can generate YunExpress labels from the same app I use for DHL and Canada Post instead of managing China shipments on a separate portal, and I can bill duties to a third-party UPS account without manual customs paperwork on every international order**.
