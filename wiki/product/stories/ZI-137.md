# Ticket #218195 — Onboarding, Product Import, TNT Integration & Product Search Issues

- **Customer**: Tina Koutas (Diff Technics)
- **Duration**: 2023-06-08 to 2023-07-27 (ongoing)

> **Note**: This ticket contains 100 comments. Only the first 3, last 10, and sampled middle comments were reviewed. Some intermediate details may be missing.

## Timeline & Key Phases

### Phase 1: Product Import & Initial Setup (2023-06-08)
- Customer imported Excel file of products; 2 of 450 products failed import
- Support re-imported the failed products successfully
- Customer asked about recurring import failures and documentation for app settings

### Phase 2: TNT Express Integration & Label Testing (2023-06-08 to 2023-06-13)
- Customer generated test orders and needed help creating TNT Express test consignments
- Issues with From Address configuration on labels
- Requested documentation on Advanced, Shipping, and Generate Labels dropdown options
- Tax invoice customization was requested and escalated to L3

### Phase 3: Shipping Rate Discrepancies (2023-06-13 to 2023-06-22)
- Customer found rate differences between TNT dashboard and checkout rates
- Products without dimensions were still generating shipping rates unexpectedly
- Encountered "Shipping not available for selected address" errors at checkout

### Phase 4: Product Search & Variant SKU Issues (2023-06-28 to 2023-07-27)
- Customer reported product search by SKU not finding valid products
- Support confirmed SKU filter does not support variants (Comment #93)
- Customer expressed strong frustration about being forced to export CSV files for variant products
- Support noted this limitation would be addressed in future updates (Comment #96: product filter option expected next month)

## Open Issues

1. **Variant SKU search not supported** — Product search filter does not support searching by variant SKU, forcing CSV export workflow for any variant product changes. Blocked: dev team. Severity: high frustration, workflow blocker. Area: product-management. (Comment #94)
2. **Product sync with Shopify** — Customer asked whether clicking "Import Products" is required to sync newly added Shopify products; search still fails to find recently added products. Blocked: dev team. Severity: moderate. Area: product-management. (Comment #100)

## Resolved Issues

1. **Failed product import** — 2 of 450 products failed import; re-imported successfully by support. (Comment #2)
2. **From Address on labels** — Resolved via App Settings -> From Address configuration. (Comment #11)
3. **TNT Express label generation** — Test labels generated successfully after setup guidance. (Comment #41)
4. **General app satisfaction** — Customer reported everything working and was asked for a review. (Comment #91)

## Customer Context

- Australian business (Kogarah, NSW) using TNT Express carrier
- High-touch onboarding with extensive back-and-forth over 7 weeks
- Products include variants with distinct SKUs
- Very vocal customer who expects prompt responses and detailed documentation
