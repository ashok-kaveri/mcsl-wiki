## User Story

As a **high-volume Indian confectionery merchant who coordinates daily with Amazon Express field executives (FEs) to resolve pickup and tracking issues, and whose MCSL manifest lacks the FE name, signature field, and pickup location details that Shiprocket includes — forcing me to spend 30+ minutes per incident manually looking up carrier assignment information**,
I want **the shipment manifest to include the assigned FE's name, a pre-printed signature field, and the pickup/seller location details so I have everything needed for carrier coordination in one document**,
so that **when 54 shipments get stuck in "Ready for Pickup" I can immediately identify and call the responsible FE instead of cross-referencing Amazon's portal, and my manifest is on par with what Shiprocket provides so I'm not paying for an inferior document**.

## Acceptance Criteria (Given/When/Then)

### Scenario: Manifest includes FE details
- **Given** an Amazon Express pickup assigned to FE "Rajesh Kumar" for 20 shipments
- **When** the merchant generates today's pickup manifest
- **Then** the manifest PDF shows "FE: Rajesh Kumar" with a signature line
- **And** pickup location and seller details are in the header

### Scenario: Bulk manifest groups by FE
- **Given** 80 shipments across 3 FE assignments for today's pickup
- **When** the daily manifest is generated
- **Then** shipments are grouped by FE with each section showing the FE's name and contact
- **And** total shipment count per FE is summarized at the top

### Scenario: FE data unavailable gracefully handled
- **Given** Amazon's API doesn't return FE assignment for some shipments
- **When** the manifest is generated
- **Then** those shipments show "FE: Not yet assigned" instead of a blank field
- **And** the manifest still generates successfully with all other details populated
