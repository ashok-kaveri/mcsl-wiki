## User Story

As a **high-volume US Shopify merchant (Dropps, 10.7k lifetime orders) who just installed MCSL on trial**,
I want **onboarding that lets me connect my carrier and generate my first label the same day I install — without waiting for PluginHive to catch me on the phone**,
so that **my trial converts based on the product working, not on whether Sibi reaches my shared sales phone line during my working hours**.

---

## Acceptance Criteria (Simple)

- [ ] Dropps connects a carrier and generates at least one rate quote before their trial expires.
- [ ] In-app self-serve onboarding guides any US merchant through carrier connection + first label without a human call.
- [ ] High-volume trialists (>5k historical orders) are flagged for proactive onboarding with multiple contact channels (email + in-app chat + call).
- [ ] Trial expiry includes an automated outreach specifically to trialists with zero carrier connected (not generic "your trial ends" email).
- [ ] Conversion funnel (install → carrier connected → first rate → first label → paid) is measurable per trialist in the admin panel.

## Acceptance Criteria (Given/When/Then)

### Scenario: Dropps engages and converts
- **Given** Dropps received the welcome email and appointment link
- **When** a followup is sent before trial expiry
- **Then** the followup is in-app (visible when they open MCSL) not just email
- **And** they can start onboarding without a phone call

### Scenario: High-volume trialist playbook
- **Given** a new trialist has >5k historical Shopify orders
- **When** they install and sit idle for 24 hours
- **Then** they're auto-flagged as a high-priority prospect
- **And** receive a personalized outreach from a named CSM plus an in-app onboarding guide

### Scenario: Self-serve conversion is possible without calls
- **Given** any trialist lands on the dashboard
- **When** they follow the guided first-run flow
- **Then** they can connect a carrier, run a test rate, and generate a test label end-to-end
- **And** the conversion funnel records each step completion for admin visibility
