## User Story

As a **new WooCommerce store owner evaluating the shipping services plugin**, I want **clear documentation and support for setting up my first carrier integration**, so that **I can quickly test the plugin with a real order before committing to a paid plan**.

---

## Acceptance Criteria (Given/When/Then)

### Scenario: WooCommerce merchant gets quick setup help

- **Given** a new WooCommerce merchant installs the plugin
- **When** they email support for onboarding help
- **Then** support responds within 4 hours with a setup guide for their first carrier
- **And** support offers a 15-minute video call if they prefer hands-on help

### Scenario: First label generation on WooCommerce succeeds

- **Given** a WooCommerce merchant has configured one carrier
- **When** they generate a label for a test order
- **Then** the label generates successfully
- **And** they can print and test carrier integration end-to-end

### Scenario: WooCommerce onboarding success drives conversion

- **Given** new WooCommerce merchants complete onboarding
- **When** we track their behavior
- **Then** 70%+ of onboarded merchants convert to paid plan within 30 days
- **And** we identify the onboarding steps that lead to conversion
