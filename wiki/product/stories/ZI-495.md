As a **high-volume German merchant shipping 500 DHL Paket labels per month who relies on the app for daily fulfillment operations**,

so that **my business doesn't lose 7 days of DHL shipping capability (117 blocked labels worth thousands in revenue), my warehouse team can continue fulfilling orders without manual carrier portal workarounds, and I have confidence that when external APIs fail, StorePep will migrate to modern alternatives rather than remaining dependent on failing infrastructure**.
