- Customer granted permission to configure app to match their existing PH Ship Rate & Track for FedEx app setup. (Comment #13)

- **DHL**: Export Declaration Surcharge (JPY 1800) incorrectly applied for Japan shipments. DHL Japan confirmed this surcharge does not apply for exports from Japan. (Comment #39)
- L3 confirmed enhancement to exclude Export Declaration for Japan shipments in upcoming release. (Comment #40)

### Phase 4: Awaiting fixes & trial extension (2026-04-02 -- 2026-04-06)
