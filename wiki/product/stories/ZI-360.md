## User Story

As a **BigCommerce merchant preparing to launch a new store after investing weeks in FedEx integration setup, rate configuration, and custom theme development**,
I want **shipping rates to display reliably at checkout regardless of what custom theme I've chosen for my storefront**,
so that **I can launch my business without having to choose between my brand's visual identity and functional shipping, and my customers can actually complete purchases instead of abandoning carts when only "pickup" appears as the sole shipping option**.

---

## Acceptance Criteria (Simple)

- [ ] Shipping rates (both live FedEx rates and flat rate automation rules) display correctly at checkout on R3 Wellness NC store with their current custom theme enabled
- [ ] App generates comprehensive compatibility check when BigCommerce merchants first connect, warning about known theme conflicts before they invest days in rate configuration
- [ ] Documentation identifies specific BigCommerce theme patterns that cause rate display issues, with step-by-step troubleshooting guide for merchants
- [ ] Support team can remotely diagnose theme conflicts from request logs without requiring merchant to provide admin access or disable their theme
- [ ] Rate display failures at checkout generate automatic alerts to support team (rather than merchant discovering after 28-day setup process)

## Acceptance Criteria (Given/When/Then)

### Scenario: Merchant with custom theme places test order
- **Given** R3 Wellness NC store has custom BigCommerce theme enabled
- **And** flat rate automation rule is configured ($9.95 based on order price)
- **When** customer adds a product to cart and proceeds to checkout
- **Then** shipping options display including the configured $9.95 flat rate
- **And** "pickup only" is NOT the sole option displayed

### Scenario: Other BigCommerce merchants with custom themes
- **Given** a BigCommerce merchant installs the FedEx app
- **And** they are using a custom theme (not BigCommerce default theme)
- **When** they complete FedEx account integration and rate configuration
- **Then** the app runs a compatibility check detecting potential theme conflicts
- **And** displays a warning message with theme-specific troubleshooting steps if conflicts detected
- **And** provides a test link to verify rates display correctly at checkout before going live

### Scenario: Proactive detection of theme conflicts
- **Given** a merchant has configured rates in the app
- **And** rates populate successfully in the request log
- **But** checkout is not displaying any shipping rates (only pickup)
- **When** this mismatch persists for 24 hours
- **Then** the system automatically creates an internal alert for support team
- **And** sends a proactive email to merchant with theme conflict troubleshooting guide
- **And** prevents 28-day escalation cycles for future merchants with similar theme conflicts
