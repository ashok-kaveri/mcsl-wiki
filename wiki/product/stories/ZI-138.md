## User Story

As a **merchant who joined PluginHive through the Amazon Shipping partnership with a pricing expectation set by Amazon, now hitting the Basic plan's label ceiling after 89 shipments in my first month**,
I want **a plan that matches the partnership's price point and includes the 400 labels/month and live tracking I actually need — without having to negotiate it one-off through email**,
so that **I can keep shipping through the app rather than churning back to the Amazon portal, and PluginHive retains the partnership-sourced customer that the partnerships team invested in acquiring**.

## Acceptance Criteria (Simple)

- [ ] Karandeep (KS International) is placed on a plan that covers 400 labels/month + live tracking at the $19/month price point, or is offered a documented alternative (grandfathered partnership rate, discount code, etc.)
- [ ] The pricing expectation set by the Amazon partnership is documented internally so any customer arriving via the same channel is offered consistent terms
- [ ] The "Label Limit Exceeded" error page shows the customer a clear next-step CTA (self-serve upgrade, chat with sales) instead of just blocking
