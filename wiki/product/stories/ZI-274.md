## User Story

As a **new Shopify merchant (Kamran) who just installed the multi-carrier shipping app**, I want **personalized guidance to configure my carriers and successfully generate my first label**, so that **I can confidently use the app to ship my next order and feel the value immediately**.

---

## Acceptance Criteria (Given/When/Then)

### Scenario: Onboarding guide walks through carrier setup

- **Given** a new merchant opens the app
- **When** they follow the "Get Started" guide
- **Then** they are guided through: (1) Select your carrier, (2) Connect your account, (3) Test a label
- **And** each step has clear examples and troubleshooting tips

### Scenario: First label generates successfully

- **Given** a new merchant has completed onboarding
- **When** they select a real order and generate a label
- **Then** the label is created without errors
- **And** they receive a confirmation with tracking information

### Scenario: Onboarding reduces trial-to-paid conversion friction

- **Given** new merchants are onboarded
- **When** we track upgrade behavior
- **Then** merchants with successful first labels upgrade to paid plans at 3x the rate of those without
- **And** average time-to-upgrade decreases from 14 days to 7 days
