# Ticket #365042 — Shipping Rates Fail for Products with 250+ Variants

- **Customer**: Abbas (4bb0c5-7d.myshopify.com / buytshirt.ca)
- **Duration**: 2025-12-17 to 2026-01-19 (ongoing)

## Timeline & Key Phases

### Phase 1: Shipping Not Working for New Products (2025-12-17)
- Customer reported shipping not working when adding new products
- Store is a Canadian t-shirt business using Purolator carrier
- Rates active, labels inactive; $29 Popular plan; installed Feb 2025 (Comment #2)
- Support requested product details and collaborator access (Comment #3)
- Merged ticket #364850 where customer shared specific products failing at checkout

### Phase 2: Root Cause Identified (2025-12-17)
- Customer clarified: products with fewer than 250 variants work; those with 300-500+ variants fail
- Example products: "Gildan 5000 - Heavy Cotton T-Shirt" with 548 variants
- Support initially showed rates working for some variants but customer demonstrated failure for specific color selections beyond the 250-variant import limit (Comment #8, #9)
- Customer escalated urgently: site is live, customers unable to place orders (Comment #10)
- Phone call with customer (3 min 59 sec) to discuss the issue (Comment #14)
- L3 confirmed: app currently supports importing only up to 250 variants per product; variants beyond this limit are not imported, causing rate failures (Comment #12)
- Existing enhancement ticket for FedEx app for the same issue; team asked to evaluate for MCSL app as well

### Phase 3: Enhancement Committed & Customer Churned (2025-12-18)
- Senior team confirmed enhancement is under development, will be released in next release (Comment #16)
- ETA: end of 2nd or 3rd week of January 2026 (Comment #18)
- Customer threatened to remove the app within 2-4 hours if not fixed (Comment #13, #15)
- Customer uninstalled the app (Comment #19)
- Uninstall follow-up email sent; support confirmed they would continue working on the enhancement (Comment #20)

### Phase 4: Development Postponed (2026-01-19)
- Since customer uninstalled, development postponed to 2nd week of February (Comment #21)

## Open Issues

1. **250-variant import limit** — App only imports up to 250 variants per product. Products exceeding this limit (e.g., 548 variants for a t-shirt with many color/size combinations) have missing variants, causing shipping rate failures at checkout. Blocked: dev team (postponed to Feb 2026 after customer churn). Severity: critical, causes checkout failures and customer churn. Area: product-management. (Comment #12)

## Resolved Issues

None. Customer uninstalled before any fix was deployed.
