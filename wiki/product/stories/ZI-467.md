## User Story

As a **business owner paying for a $29/month shipping app subscription**,
I want **to receive immediate confirmation when my subscription activates and be protected from duplicate charges if I retry the payment flow**,
so that **I don't get overcharged $58 due to missing confirmation emails, and I don't have to wait days for a refund while my business cash flow is impacted**.

---

## Acceptance Criteria (Simple)

- [ ] True Pump & Equipment (Visa ending 4994) receives $58 refund for the two duplicate charges from 4/20 and 4/21
- [ ] Subscription activation flow sends immediate confirmation email when payment succeeds (before page redirect/refresh)
- [ ] If customer retries subscription activation within 5 minutes, system detects existing active subscription and prevents duplicate charge
- [ ] Payment gateway (Stripe/PayPal) is configured with idempotency keys to prevent double-charging on retry/refresh
- [ ] All subscription activation attempts (success, failure, duplicate) are logged with timestamp, customer ID, payment method, result

## Acceptance Criteria (Given/When/Then)

### Scenario: Refund duplicate charges for True Pump & Equipment
- **Given** True Pump & Equipment has 3 charges on Visa ending 4994: 4/20 $29, 4/20 $29 (dup), 4/21 $29 (dup)
- **When** L3 verifies duplicate charges
- **Then** two duplicate charges ($58 total) are refunded to Visa ending 4994
- **And** customer receives confirmation email with refund details and timeline (3-5 business days)

### Scenario: Immediate confirmation email after subscription activation
- **Given** a merchant completes subscription activation and payment succeeds
- **When** payment gateway returns success response
- **Then** confirmation email is sent immediately (before page redirect) with: plan name, amount charged, billing date, receipt link, next billing date
- **And** user sees success message on screen: "Subscription activated! Confirmation email sent to [email]"

### Scenario: Prevent duplicate charges on retry within 5 minutes
- **Given** a merchant activates subscription at 10:00 AM and payment succeeds
- **When** merchant clicks "Activate" button again at 10:02 AM (due to slow page load / no confirmation / confusion)
- **Then** system checks for active subscription created in last 5 minutes
- **And** displays message: "Subscription already active as of 10:00 AM. Check your email for confirmation."
- **And** no additional charge is processed
