# Ticket #387062 — Multiple Post-Onboarding Bugs: FedEx Rate Failures, Address Line Duplication on Labels, Product Sync Not Automatic, and DHL Invoice Missing Freight Cost

- **Customer**: Rinaldo Hollat (eurosec.myshopify.com)
- **Product**: Shopify Multi Carrier Shipping Label
- **Duration**: 2026-05-01 to 2026-06-18 (49 days, ongoing)

## Timeline & Key Phases

### Phase 1: Onboarding (2026-05-01 to 2026-05-04)
- App installed on eurosec.myshopify.com; FedEx REST carrier added during initial setup
- Outbound call placed by support (RNR); Zoom onboarding scheduled for May 4
- Onboarding session covered: rate automation rules for FedEx and DHL Express, label generation workflow, and pickup request options
- Customer was generating labels directly via carrier portal before this; session demonstrated app-based label generation

### Phase 2: FedEx Rates Not Displaying at Checkout (2026-05-07 to 2026-05-20)
- Customer reported FedEx rates not showing at checkout within first 10 orders; backup rate was applied instead
- Support confirmed a known bug causing delays in FedEx rate display; fix deployed by end of following week (Comment #9)
- Engineering confirmed fix live on 2026-05-20 (Comment #10); support verified rates displaying correctly on customer store (Comment #12)
- Customer acknowledged; issue considered resolved

### Phase 3: Order Item Sync After Shopify Order Edit (2026-05-20 to 2026-05-22)
- Customer reported edited orders (item replacements) not syncing correctly — only changed items were imported, not retained items (Comment #13)
- Support escalated to L3 to enable "auto order updates" toggle for the store (Comment #18)
- Feature toggle enabled on 2026-05-22 (Comment #22); customer informed (Comment #23)

### Phase 4: Address Line Duplication on Labels and Commercial Invoices — Multiple Failed Fixes (2026-05-20 to 2026-06-02)
- Customer reported Address Line 1 and Address Line 2 being merged and then duplicated on shipping labels and CIs (Comment #13)
- Support initially claimed it was resolved (Comment #17); customer pushed back with new evidence (Comment #34, #37)
- L3 escalation raised; QA-confirmed reproduction: when both address lines are present, they are combined and printed twice (Comment #39)
- Fix deployed 2026-05-26 (Comment #40); customer confirmed still broken (Comment #43)
- Second fix deployed 2026-05-28 (Comment #53); customer confirmed still broken (Comment #56)
- Third L3 escalation raised (Comment #58); engineering confirmed root cause as Shopify Flow timing interaction with order import (Comment #68)
- Third fix confirmed live 2026-06-02 (Comment #68); support unable to test in QA store due to FedEx API downtime; customer requested to provide test order (Comment #71)
- As of last follow-up, no explicit customer confirmation of resolution — address line issue appears unresolved or unverified

### Phase 5: Product Details Not Auto-Syncing from Shopify (2026-05-21 to present)
- Customer reported that weight, HS Code, and Country of Origin updated in Shopify are not syncing to the app automatically; only "Force Import" works (Comment #24)
- Support reproduced the issue (Comment #31, #32); L3 raised (Comment #32)
- Engineering identified the issue and began work on a fix (Comment #33); no public confirmation of fix deployed as of latest comments

### Phase 6: Order Import Failures for Orders in Shopify Flow "On Hold" State (2026-06-01 to 2026-06-03)
- Customer reported only 5 of 10 orders imported; some "On Hold" orders imported, others did not (Comment #60, #62)
- Engineering explained the race condition: Shopify Flow marking orders On Hold before webhook triggers causes orders to import without line items, routing them to "Not To Ship" (Comment #68, #71)
- Workaround provided: use manual Import Orders button after releasing Hold; acknowledged as a platform limitation, not a product fix

### Phase 7: DHL Invoice Missing Freight Cost (2026-06-03 to present)
- Customer reported that the "Include Shipping Charges in Invoice" setting in DHL carrier config is enabled, but freight cost is absent from generated DHL invoices (Comment #72)
- DHL portal can include freight cost; customer provided sample invoice and screenshot of portal option (Comment #74, #76)
- L3 raised (Comment #78); engineering confirmed feasibility check needed (Comment #79)
- Confirmed: freight charges will be added to DHL invoice in MCSL 383 release, ETA on or before 7 July (Comment #80, #82)

## Open Issues

1. **DHL Commercial Invoice Does Not Include Freight Cost Despite Setting Being Enabled** — Customer has the "Include Shipping Charges in Invoice" option enabled in DHL carrier settings, but generated invoices omit the freight cost. This is required for customs clearance on international DHL shipments, making DHL unusable for international orders. Blocked: customer (cannot use DHL internationally until fixed). Severity: high — feature setting exists in UI but has no effect; customer explicitly states DHL is not usable without this. Engineering confirmed enhancement scheduled for MCSL 383, ETA 7 July 2026. Area: label-generation, international. (Comment #72, #78, #80, #82)

2. **Product Details (Weight, HS Code, Country of Origin) Do Not Auto-Sync from Shopify** — Updates to product attributes in Shopify are not propagating to the app automatically; customer must use Force Import every time. L3 escalation confirmed by engineering that a fix is in progress but no deployment confirmation has been made. Blocked: customer (manual workaround required for every product update). Severity: medium — functional workaround exists but workflow is broken for customers managing product data in Shopify. Area: product-management. (Comment #24, #32, #33)

3. **Address Line Duplication on Labels and Commercial Invoices — Unconfirmed Resolution** — When both Address Line 1 and Address Line 2 are populated in the recipient or sender address, both lines are combined and printed twice on shipping labels and CIs. Three fix attempts were made (2026-05-26, 2026-05-28, 2026-06-02); customer confirmed persistence after the first two. After the third fix, support could not verify in QA due to FedEx API downtime, and no explicit customer confirmation was obtained. Status is ambiguous — the issue may or may not be resolved. Blocked: customer / support (unverified). Severity: high — affects all labels and invoices for addresses with two address lines. Area: label-generation. (Comment #13, #39, #51, #56, #68, #71)

## Resolved Issues

1. **FedEx Rates Not Displaying at Checkout** — Rates were not showing at checkout, causing backup rates to be applied. A known platform-wide bug was confirmed by engineering; fix deployed and verified on 2026-05-20. Customer confirmed rates were displaying correctly. (Comment #9, #10, #11)

2. **Order Items Not Syncing Correctly After Shopify Order Edit** — When a Shopify order was edited (items replaced), the app only imported the changed items, missing the unchanged items. Resolved by enabling the "auto order updates" account-level toggle for the store. (Comment #17, #22, #23)

3. **Shopify Flow "On Hold" Orders Not Importing — Root Cause Explained** — Some On Hold orders were not appearing in the app. Engineering identified a race condition between Shopify Flow execution and the order import webhook. Workaround documented: use manual Import Orders button after releasing Hold status. Not a code fix — behaviour is expected given current architecture. (Comment #68, #71)

## User Story

As a **security product retailer in Estonia shipping internationally via DHL Express with commercial invoices required for customs clearance**,
I want **freight charges to appear on DHL commercial invoices generated by the app, matching what the DHL portal itself can produce**,
so that **my international shipments clear customs without delays or rejections, and I can stop manually creating invoices through the DHL portal for every non-EU order**.

## Acceptance Criteria (Simple)

- [ ] When "Include Shipping Charges in Invoice" is enabled in DHL carrier settings, the generated DHL commercial invoice includes the freight cost as a line item
- [ ] The freight cost value on the invoice matches the actual DHL shipping charge for the shipment
- [ ] Invoices for eurosec.myshopify.com (ticket #387062) correctly display freight cost after the fix is deployed
- [ ] DHL invoices without "Include Shipping Charges in Invoice" enabled remain unchanged (no freight cost shown)
- [ ] The freight cost field is transmitted to DHL's API in the correct payload structure so the carrier-side invoice also reflects it

## Acceptance Criteria (Given/When/Then)

### Scenario: Freight cost appears on DHL commercial invoice when setting is enabled
- **Given** a store has DHL Express configured with "Include Shipping Charges in Invoice" enabled
- **When** the merchant generates a DHL international shipping label with a commercial invoice
- **Then** the commercial invoice includes a freight cost line item matching the shipment's DHL shipping charge

### Scenario: Freight cost is omitted when setting is disabled
- **Given** a store has DHL Express configured with "Include Shipping Charges in Invoice" disabled
- **When** the merchant generates a DHL international shipping label with a commercial invoice
- **Then** the commercial invoice does not include a freight cost line item

### Scenario: Freight cost is included in batch label generation
- **Given** a store has DHL Express configured with "Include Shipping Charges in Invoice" enabled and multiple international orders selected
- **When** the merchant generates DHL labels in batch
- **Then** each generated commercial invoice includes the correct freight cost for its respective shipment
