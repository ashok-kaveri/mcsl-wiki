# Ticket #382961 — Orders with post-purchase upsell move to Not to Ship on refresh

- **Customer**: Helen (Brewlala — brewlala.com.au)
- **Product**: Shopify Multi Carrier Shipping Label
- **Duration**: 2026-04-08 to 2026-04-09 (2 days, ongoing)

## Timeline & Key Phases

### Phase 1: Initial Report (2026-04-08)
- Customer reported orders disappearing when using One Click Upsell (OCU) post-purchase flow. Specifically, order #1880 moved to "Not to Ship" after refresh.
- Support (Priyanka) requested detailed explanation and test orders.
- Customer created four test orders covering all OCU scenarios: #1882 (no upsell), #1883 (in-cart upsell), #1884 (post-purchase upsell), #1885 (both upsells).
- Customer confirmed the issue triggers when clicking the orange refresh icon after a post-purchase upsell modifies the order in Shopify.

### Phase 2: L3 Escalation and Investigation (2026-04-09)
- Support escalated to L3 engineering with full reproduction details and Slack thread link.
- L3 (Veena) moved all 4 test orders to INITIAL state and reimported products. Asked merchant to share future order IDs without making changes. (Comment #14)
- Support (Priyanka) reproduced the issue independently: Order #1883 also moved to "Not to Ship" on refresh despite having products present in the app. Escalated back to L3 noting the issue is broader than just post-purchase upsell orders. (Comment #15)

## Open Issues

1. **Orders move to "Not to Ship" on refresh when order has post-purchase upsell line items** — When a Shopify order includes a post-purchase upsell product (added via One Click Upsell app after checkout), clicking the refresh icon in MCSL moves the order from the active orders list to "Not to Ship". The OCU post-purchase flow adds a new line item and payment after initial checkout, which appears to trigger MCSL's order-update logic to reclassify the order. Blocked: customer (cannot process these orders). Severity: high (orders silently disappear from fulfillment queue, affects all post-purchase upsell orders). Area: order-management. (Comment #1, #4, #13)

2. **Refresh issue also affects non-post-purchase upsell orders** — Support reproduced the problem on order #1883 (in-cart upsell only, no post-purchase), which also moved to "Not to Ship" on refresh despite both products existing in the app. This suggests the root cause may be broader than just OCU post-purchase modifications. Blocked: engineering (needs further investigation). Severity: high (scope of affected orders may be larger than initially reported). Area: order-management. (Comment #15)

## Resolved Issues

None — investigation is ongoing.

## User Story

As a **small-volume Australian coffee merchant on a $19 plan who relies on a third-party upsell app to increase average order value**,
I want **my orders to remain in the active fulfillment queue regardless of how Shopify line items were added — whether at checkout, via in-cart upsell, or post-purchase upsell**,
so that **I stop losing revenue from orders that silently vanish into "Not to Ship" and go unshipped until a customer complains, forcing me to manually hunt through filtered views to find and rescue them every day**.

---

## Acceptance Criteria (Simple)

- [ ] Orders containing post-purchase upsell line items (added via OCU or similar apps after initial checkout) remain in the active orders list after clicking the refresh icon
- [ ] Orders containing in-cart upsell line items remain in the active orders list after clicking the refresh icon, matching the behavior confirmed broken on order #1883
- [ ] The order-update logic correctly reconciles Shopify orders where line items and payments were added after the initial checkout event
- [ ] Orders that were previously moved to "Not to Ship" due to this bug can be bulk-recovered back to the active fulfillment queue without manual intervention per order
- [ ] A regression test covers the scenario where an order's line items change between initial import and a subsequent refresh (line item count mismatch should not trigger Not-to-Ship classification)

---

## Acceptance Criteria (Given/When/Then)

### Scenario: Post-purchase upsell order stays active after refresh
- **Given** a Shopify order exists with an original line item and a post-purchase upsell line item added by the One Click Upsell app after checkout
- **When** the operator clicks the orange refresh icon on the MCSL order grid for that order
- **Then** the order remains in the active orders list with all line items visible and does not move to "Not to Ship"

### Scenario: In-cart upsell order stays active after refresh
- **Given** a Shopify order exists with both a primary product and an in-cart upsell product, where all products are imported into the MCSL app
- **When** the operator clicks the refresh icon on the MCSL order grid for that order
- **Then** the order remains in the active orders list and retains its existing fulfillment status without reclassification

### Scenario: Bulk recovery of orders incorrectly classified as Not-to-Ship
- **Given** multiple orders were previously moved to "Not to Ship" due to the upsell refresh bug and are visible in the Not-to-Ship filtered view
- **When** an operator selects those orders and triggers a bulk move back to the active fulfillment queue
- **Then** all selected orders return to the active orders list with their original line items intact and ready for label generation
