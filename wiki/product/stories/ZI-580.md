## User Story

As a **Magento merchant on a $49 Premium plan shipping lightweight specialty products from the Netherlands to Sweden, whose PostNL contract includes service code 6942 (Boxable Track & Trace) for tracked letterbox delivery — and who has been overpaying for full parcel services (code 3085) for 73 days because the app doesn't expose the cheaper service that PostNL's own integrations team recommended**,
I want **PostNL service code 6942 mapped and available in MCSL so I can ship letterbox-sized packages using my contracted tracked service at the negotiated rate**,
so that **I stop overpaying on every lightweight order, I can use the service PostNL specifically told me to use, and I don't have to explain to PostNL's Channel Integrations team why their recommended service code doesn't work in my shipping app after 73 days of waiting**.
