## User Story

As a **new store owner on the $19 Starter plan preparing for launch, who imported my entire product catalog from my previous platform only to discover a hard product limit in the app blocking my go-live**,
I want **transparent product limits communicated upfront during onboarding (before I import 500 products), automatic plan upgrade suggestions when I hit the limit ("Your Starter plan supports 100 products. Upgrade to Popular for 1,000 products: $29/month"), and self-service limit increase for paying customers without requiring a support ticket**,
so that **I don't waste 2 days waiting for support to escalate a limit increase that should be instant, don't miss my store launch date because shipping isn't configured, and don't feel blindsided by arbitrary limits that weren't mentioned anywhere during installation**.

---

## Acceptance Criteria (Simple)

- [ ] Product limits are clearly displayed during app installation: "Starter plan: 100 products, Popular plan: 1,000 products, Advanced plan: Unlimited"
- [ ] When merchant reaches 80% of product limit: in-app notification suggests upgrade before hitting hard limit
- [ ] When merchant hits product limit: error message shows current plan limit and "Upgrade Now" button (instant upgrade, no support ticket)
- [ ] Merchants on paid plans can request limit increase via self-service form (approved within 1 business day for reasonable requests)
- [ ] App settings page shows: "Product Usage: 85/100 products synced. Upgrade to Popular for 1,000 products."

---

## Acceptance Criteria (Given/When/Then)

### Scenario: Merchant hits product limit during import
- **Given** merchant is on Starter plan (100 product limit)
- **And** merchant imports CSV with 150 products
- **When** import reaches product 100
- **Then** import pauses and displays: "You've reached your Starter plan limit (100 products). To import 50 more products, upgrade to Popular ($29/month, supports 1,000 products). [Upgrade Now] [Continue with 100 products]"
- **And** if merchant clicks "Upgrade Now", plan upgrades instantly and import resumes

### Scenario: Merchant receives proactive warning before hitting limit
- **Given** merchant is on Starter plan with 80 products synced (80% of 100 limit)
- **When** merchant logs into app
- **Then** banner displays: "You're using 80/100 products (80%). Consider upgrading to Popular plan (1,000 products) before adding more inventory."
- **And** banner is dismissible but reappears when merchant reaches 90 products

### Scenario: Merchant on paid plan requests custom limit increase
- **Given** merchant is on Advanced plan (default 1,000 product limit) with 950 products
- **And** merchant needs to add 200 more products for seasonal catalog expansion
- **When** merchant navigates to Settings → Product Limits → Request Increase
- **Then** self-service form appears: "Current limit: 1,000. Requested limit: [input]. Business justification: [textarea]. Submit for review (approved within 1 business day)."
- **And** merchant receives email confirmation: "Limit increase request received. Current limit remains 1,000 until approved. Expected response: 1 business day."
