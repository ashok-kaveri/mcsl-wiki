## Open Issues

1. **Orders move to "Not to Ship" on refresh when order has post-purchase upsell line items** — When a Shopify order includes a post-purchase upsell product (added via One Click Upsell app after checkout), clicking the refresh icon in MCSL moves the order from the active orders list to "Not to Ship". The OCU post-purchase flow adds a new line item and payment after initial checkout, which appears to trigger MCSL's order-update logic to reclassify the order. Blocked: customer (cannot process these orders). Severity: high (orders silently disappear from fulfillment queue, affects all post-purchase upsell orders). Area: order-management. (Comment #1, #4, #13)

2. **Refresh issue also affects non-post-purchase upsell orders** — Support reproduced the problem on order #1883 (in-cart upsell only, no post-purchase), which also moved to "Not to Ship" on refresh despite both products existing in the app. This suggests the root cause may be broader than just OCU post-purchase modifications. Blocked: engineering (needs further investigation). Severity: high (scope of affected orders may be larger than initially reported). Area: order-management. (Comment #15)
