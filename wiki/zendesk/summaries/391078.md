## Open Issues

1. **MCSL: FedEx REST rates fail with `postalCode can not be null` for no-postal-code destinations (HK, Taiwan, likely others)** — When the destination locale does not use postal codes (HK SAR, Taiwan, possibly other regions), MCSL omits the postal-code node in the FedEx REST rate request; FedEx rejects with `postalCode can not be null`. Fix is to inject the FedEx-required placeholder (e.g., `000000`) automatically for no-postal-code countries. Blocked: engineering (dev_needed; ETA 1st week of June). Severity: P1 — customer cannot ship to HK/Taiwan via MCSL; has resorted to manual dual-routing through DHL+FedEx for HK. Area: `international`. (Comments #1, #2, #4, #5, #6, #8)

2. **Cross-app consistency: same FedEx-REST/no-postal-code bug also tracked on the Shopify FedEx app (#387886)** — Identical root cause was raised by Morrison (Belgium) on the standalone Shopify FedEx app on 2026-05-11. Fix must land in both code paths (MCSL and the FedEx-app), and the ETAs should be aligned to avoid one merchant being unblocked while the other waits. Blocked: engineering (cross-app coordination). Severity: P2 — coordination/QA. Area: `international`. (Comment #5 here + ticket [#387886](387886.md))
