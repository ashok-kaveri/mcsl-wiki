# Ticket #394243 — SLGP Page Refresh Bug and Product Page UX Gaps for A&S Welding

- **Customer**: David Barkho / A&S Welding & Electrical Repairs and Supplies (aandswelding.myshopify.com)
- **Product**: Shopify Multi Carrier Shipping Label
- **Duration**: 2026-06-09 to 2026-06-18 (9 days, ongoing)

## Timeline & Key Phases

### Phase 1: Initial Feature Requests (2026-06-09 to 2026-06-10)
- Customer requested two things via live chat: (1) default the Orders view to "Open" instead of "All", and (2) auto-update orders when items are added/removed.
- L3 escalated both. Auto order update toggle was enabled immediately.
- Product team requested workflow details from customer to evaluate the default Open Orders view request.
- Customer provided detailed justification: 2–3 fulfilment batches/day, switching views every session adds cognitive load and 1–2 minutes per batch.

### Phase 2: SLGP Upsell and Enablement (2026-06-10 to 2026-06-11)
- L3 suggested SLGP (Single Label Generation Process) as a solution to the Open Orders default view request, similar to "ruggedradios" case.
- Two calls made with customer. During first call, SLGP was demonstrated and customer was interested.
- Customer was on $9 Starter plan; SLGP required $99 Enterprise plan. Customer declined — said $49/$99 too expensive, agreed to upgrade to $29 Popular Plan.
- Customer upgraded to $29 Popular Plan and SLGP was enabled (Comment #21).
- Additional product page feedback captured during call (navigation state loss, per-item dimension bulk edit).

### Phase 3: SLGP Testing and Bug Discovery (2026-06-14 to 2026-06-17)
- Customer tested SLGP and confirmed label generation and fulfilment worked, but identified a UI bug: after "Generate Label and Fulfil", the button does not change from "Generate Label" to "Print Documents" — customer must manually refresh the page, which then lands them on All Orders, requiring an extra click back to the order.
- Customer also reported a split-fulfilment issue: when part of an order is "On Hold" and part is "Unfulfilled", SLGP fulfilment fails with "Invalid fulfillment order line item quantity requested" error.
- Zoom call held 2026-06-17 to reproduce both issues.
- L3 identified the page-refresh issue and confirmed a fix; ETA 7th July (Comment #47).
- L3 flagged the partial fulfilment feature is not enabled for this store as suspected cause of split-order SLGP failure.
- Safari browser document printing issue also surfaced during call.

### Phase 4: Partial Fix Deployed, Awaiting Verification (2026-06-18)
- Team confirmed the page-not-refreshing issue was addressed and asked customer to verify with new orders.
- Safari print issue and remaining items pending customer verification and further dev work.

## Open Issues

1. **SLGP Page Does Not Refresh After Label Generation** — After "Generate Label and Fulfil" completes, the order summary page does not auto-refresh; the button remains "Generate Label" instead of transitioning to "Print Documents". Customer must manually refresh, which redirects to All Orders, requiring an extra navigation step back to the order — negating the SLGP efficiency gain. A fix was identified and reportedly deployed (Comment #45), but customer has not yet confirmed. ETA for full resolution: on or before 7th July. Blocked: customer awaiting verification. Severity: High — directly breaks the core SLGP workflow the customer upgraded their plan for. Area: label-generation. (Comments #26, #38, #39, #47)

2. **SLGP Fails for Partially Fulfilled Orders (Split Fulfilment)** — When an order has some items "On Hold" and remaining items "Unfulfilled" (split via Shopify Admin), SLGP label generation succeeds but fulfilment fails with "Invalid fulfillment order line item quantity requested". Partial fulfilment feature is not enabled for this store and is the suspected root cause. L3 noted this needs enabling (Comment #38) but no confirmation it was done. Blocked: customer/engineering. Severity: High — customer uses split fulfilment regularly and cannot complete SLGP flow for these orders. Area: label-generation. (Comments #35, #38)

3. **Print Documents in Safari Browser Fails After Label Generation** — Customer reported a print/document issue specific to Safari browser after SLGP fulfilment. Raised during 2026-06-17 call; team asked customer to first verify the page-refresh fix before investigating this separately. Not yet confirmed or reproduced. Blocked: customer (pending verification of fix first). Severity: Medium. Area: label-generation. (Comment #46)

4. **Products Page Loses Search/Filter State on Back Navigation** — When a customer searches for a product, opens it from the Products page, edits it, and navigates back, the app returns to the default All Orders page instead of restoring the previous search/filter state. Customer must repeat the search. Flagged to product team; no commitment or ETA given. Blocked: product team. Severity: Medium — repeated friction for merchants managing large product catalogues. Area: product-management. (Comments #19, #20)

5. **Products Page Lacks Per-Item Dimension Bulk Edit** — Existing bulk edit for dimensions works only when all selected products share the same dimensions. Customer requires the ability to select multiple products/variants and assign different dimensions to each in a single workflow. Flagged to product team; no commitment or ETA given. Blocked: product team. Severity: Medium. Area: product-management. (Comments #19, #20)

## Resolved Issues

1. **Auto Order Update Not Enabled** — Customer requested auto-update for orders when items are added/removed. L3 enabled the Auto Order Update toggle for the store immediately. Customer was informed and accepted. (Comment #6, #8)

2. **SLGP Not Enabled on Starter Plan** — Customer was on $9 Starter plan and ineligible for SLGP. After product demo on call, customer upgraded to $29 Popular Plan and SLGP was enabled. (Comments #19, #21, #22)

3. **Default Open Orders View Request — Deprioritised** — Customer requested Orders to default to "Open" view. Team evaluated and decided not to implement this, offering SLGP as an alternative workflow. Customer accepted SLGP as a workaround and has not followed up on this specific request. (Comments #24, #30, #33)
