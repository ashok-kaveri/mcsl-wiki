  - **MCSL**: when phone is missing at checkout, MCSL currently passes the value as `null` — needs input sanitisation and a warning/error when recipient phone is not available
- Moved to `dev_needed` for both apps

Blocked: engineering (dev_needed). Severity: P1 — same regulatory deadline. Area: `carrier-config`. (Comment #3)

3. **Opt-out flow for AusPost non-essential tracking notifications** — AusPost notice also covers ability for recipients to opt out of non-essential tracking notifications, effective same date. Not yet broken down into engineering tasks. Blocked: product analysis (scope to be defined). Severity: P2 regulatory — likely needed for full compliance with AusPost partner requirements by 4 Aug 2026. Area: `tracking`. (Comment #1)
