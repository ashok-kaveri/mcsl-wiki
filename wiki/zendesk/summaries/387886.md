## Open Issues

Workaround (manually enter `000000` in Shopify) doesn't work because Shopify hides the field for HK. Engineering committed a permanent fix (likely auto-inject the FedEx-required placeholder when the destination country uses no postal codes); ETA has now slipped at least twice. Blocked: engineering (dev_needed). Severity: P1 — customer cannot ship HK orders via the app, has been blocked for 7+ days, very vocal escalation (`high_agent_replies` tag). Area: `international`.
