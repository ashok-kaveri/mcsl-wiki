2. **Label stock 4x6 not available in REST** -- REST integration lost the Stock 4x6 label format option that existed in legacy. FedEx cannot scan labels in the new smaller format. (Comment #24, #29)
3. **Auto-upload documents failing in REST** -- Post-upload of commercial invoices/customs documents fails with REST API. Under investigation with FedEx team. (Comment #20, #29, #34)

## Resolved Issues

2. **Automation rules migration** -- Customer migrated all automation rules to new FedEx REST accounts; verified by agent. (Comment #19)
