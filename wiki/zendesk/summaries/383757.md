# Ticket #383757 — FedEx ZPL format + multi-currency commercial invoice request

- **Customer**: Thomas / LOUIS XVI Team (corsin.jost@louisxviwatches.com) — Switzerland store
- **Product**: Shopify Multi Carrier Shipping Label App
- **Duration**: 2026-04-13 (ongoing, same day)

## Timeline & Key Phases

### Phase 1: FedEx label format complaint (2026-04-13 morning)
- Customer reports FedEx label options are limited to PDF or Print, both of which only cover about half the page (unusable for their thermal printer). ZPL option is not available for FedEx; UPS labels with ZPL work correctly (Comment #0).
- Support (Touseef) suggests switching image type to PNG for thermal printing. Customer confirms PNG works for FedEx (Comments #2, #3).

### Phase 2: Currency-on-commercial-invoice request (2026-04-13 afternoon)
- Customer raises a second issue: commercial invoices and product values on shipping documents always show store base currency (EUR). Customer wants documents to show the destination/recipient currency (they accept payments in 10+ currencies) (Comment #3).
- Support asks what currency international orders are placed in; requests example orders and Collaborator Access to the store (Comment #4).
- Customer shares test-order screenshots (order placed from US, invoice still in EUR instead of USD) and grants Collaborator Access (Comment #5). Awaiting PluginHive investigation.

## Open Issues

1. **Commercial invoice currency stuck at store base currency (EUR)** — Customer wants shipping documents / commercial invoices to reflect the order's destination-country currency (e.g., USD for US orders) rather than the store default. App appears to always use store base currency. Reproduction confirmed with a test order placed from US (invoice shows EUR). Blocked: PluginHive to review via collaborator access — may need product change if currency conversion isn't currently supported. Severity: M (compliance concern for a multi-currency international business). Area: `international`. (Comment #5)

## Resolved Issues

1. **FedEx label formatting (thermal printer)** — Resolved by switching FedEx image type to PNG, which produces a thermal-printer-compatible label. ZPL is not offered for FedEx; PNG was the recommended workaround. (Comments #2, #3)

- Store: LOUIS XVI (Switzerland), $19 plan, active from 2026-04-09.
- Volume: 316 orders; 2 labels generated in April 2026.
- Carriers: UPS, FedEx (uses both rates and labels).
- Business profile: Watch retailer shipping internationally; accepts 10+ currencies for international orders.
- Access: Collaborator Access granted on 2026-04-13 for investigation.
