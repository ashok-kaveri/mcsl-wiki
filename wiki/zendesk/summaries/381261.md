## Timeline & Key Phases

### Phase 1: Initial setup issues (2026-03-28)
- Customer reported DHL checkout display name and estimated delivery showing incorrectly. Agent identified these were coming from the separate "DHL Commerce" app, not the PluginHive app. Resolved by enabling PluginHive DHL rates. (Comment #2, #4, #5)

### Phase 2: Rate mismatch discovery (2026-03-28 -- 2026-03-30)
- Customer found shipping rates from the app did not match actual carrier pricing for both FedEx and DHL. App was using Weight Based Packing instead of accounting for product dimensions/volume. (Comment #7, #8)
- Agent explained packing methods; customer frustrated after two weeks of setup struggles. (Comment #9, #11)
- Customer granted permission to configure app to match their existing PH Ship Rate & Track for FedEx app setup. (Comment #13)

### Phase 3: Zoom call & rate investigation (2026-03-31 -- 2026-04-02)
- Zoom call conducted; two rate issues identified. (Comment #27)
- **FedEx**: Insurance surcharge incorrectly added to international shipments. Team contacted FedEx for clarification.
- **DHL**: Export Declaration Surcharge (JPY 1800) incorrectly applied for Japan shipments. DHL Japan confirmed this surcharge does not apply for exports from Japan. (Comment #39)
- L3 confirmed enhancement to exclude Export Declaration for Japan shipments in upcoming release. (Comment #40)

### Phase 4: Awaiting fixes & trial extension (2026-04-02 -- 2026-04-06)
- Customer asked for ETA; none available. (Comment #42, #44)
- Customer requested trial extension due to app not functioning properly; 14-day extension granted. (Comment #45, #46)
- FedEx rates mismatch resolved in app. DHL fix still pending. (Comment #46)
