# Ticket #384856 — Multi-carrier WooCommerce setup blocked by shipping zone blank-screen bug and packaging automation limitations

- **Customer**: Jarrod Clark / Central Coast Websites (aboutbunting.com.au)
- **Product**: WooCommerce Shipping Services (WSS) — multi-carrier plugin (TNT Australia + Australia Post MPB)
- **Duration**: 2026-04-20 to 2026-06-18 (60 days, ongoing)

## Timeline & Key Phases

### Phase 1: Pre-sale evaluation and carrier onboarding (2026-04-20 – 2026-04-29)
- Web developer (Jarrod) contacted support on behalf of client (About Bunting) asking about bundling TNT, AusPost, and FedEx plugins and fuel surcharge handling.
- Directed from individual plugins to WooCommerce Shipping Services (WSS) multi-carrier solution.
- TNT authentication initially failed — resolved by obtaining correct API credentials from client.
- First Zoom call (2026-04-29): incorrect WooCommerce dimension/weight units caused rate failures at checkout; fixed on call. TNT AU and AusPost MPB connected for rate display.

### Phase 2: Rate zone configuration and product quantity limit (2026-05-01 – 2026-05-13)
- Customer requested postcode-based carrier filtering (TNT for some zones, AusPost for others).
- Bulk postcode entry via Excel copy-paste confirmed to work (Comments #23, #25).
- Trial expiry surfaced: shipping stopped showing rates — partly due to trial grace period, partly due to 350-unit checkout quantity cap being exceeded (client's products sold by meter, 400 units in cart).
- L3 escalation (Comment #32): quantity limit increased to 3000 for account e81ea929-ee11-4c22-87a5-a970b420e4fa. Rates restored (Comments #33–#34).
- Customer confirmed working; client purchased plugin.

### Phase 3: Post-purchase zone and rate accuracy issues (2026-05-21 – 2026-05-26)
- After purchase, shipping zones stopped working: page went blank on clicking existing zone.
- Fix: recreate zone (workaround, Comments #40–#41).
- Persistent issue: rate automation rules not triggering for postcode zones.
- Root cause: "Match All Conditions" inadvertently enabled on zone, and leading spaces in pasted postcodes preventing matches. L3 escalation (Comment #56). Veena reconfigured postal codes (Comment #57). Rates restored.
- Separate rate accuracy issue: AusPost Express showing $30.85 vs carrier quote of $221.29 for large orders — traced to Weight Based Packing method ignoring dimensions. Customer unable to switch to box packing due to non-linear product structure (meters-as-quantity).

### Phase 4: Packaging limitation acknowledged and live site deployment (2026-05-27 – 2026-06-01)
- Zoom call (2026-05-29): support acknowledged the customer's packaging requirement (single product across multiple variable-size boxes, products sold by meter) cannot be fully automated with current packaging logic. Suggested cost adjustment as workaround (Comment #79).
- Customer duplicated staging to live site; plugin remained synced to staging — no rates on live site.
- Fix: resynced plugin to live domain (Comment #85). Rates appeared on live site.

### Phase 5: Blank screen bug on shipping zone page — open (2026-06-01 – 2026-06-18)
- Blank screen recurs when editing automation/shipping zones on live site (aboutbunting.com.au).
- L3 raised (Comment #88): noted this bug was previously fixed in MCSL app but is now present in WSS plugin.
- Workaround provided: create new zone. Engineering fix ETA: on or before 7th July 2026 (Comment #91).

## Open Issues

1. **Shipping zone page renders blank on edit** — Navigating to an existing shipping zone in the WSS plugin causes the page to go blank, preventing the customer from editing postcode configurations without recreating zones from scratch. Blocked: customer (cannot manage zones independently on live store). Severity: High — customer is live and needs to adjust zones post-launch; the recreate-zone workaround is destructive and does not scale. Area: carrier-config. (Comments #86, #88, #89, #91)

2. **Packaging automation cannot handle variable-box, meter-sold products** — Customer's products (bunting, flags, stickers) are sold by meter with quantity representing meters; box size varies non-linearly with quantity. Current Box Packing and Quantity Based Packing methods cannot map different quantity ranges to different box sizes per product type. Blocked: customer (must use manual cost adjustment as workaround; rate accuracy is not achievable). Severity: Medium — known limitation acknowledged by support on call; customer accepted the workaround but the underlying gap persists. Area: rate-shopping. (Comment #79)
