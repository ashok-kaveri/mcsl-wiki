# Ticket #392755 — Auto-populate recipient Tax ID from Shopify order notes into shipping/billing address fields

- **Customer**: Wako Chapurlat / MAJO GARN (majo-garn.myshopify.com)

- **Duration**: 2026-05-29 to 2026-06-18 (21 days, ongoing)

## Timeline & Key Phases

### Phase 1: Feature Request Submitted (2026-05-29)
- Merchant requests automatic sync of customer Tax ID (VAT number) from Shopify into the TAX ID field in PH Multi Carrier shipping and billing address sections.
- Currently, TAX ID field is always empty in the app even when the data exists in Shopify, requiring manual entry per shipment.
- Support asks for an order example and screenshot to understand data location. (Comment #3)

### Phase 2: Data Source Clarification (2026-05-29 to 2026-06-01)
- Merchant clarifies: Tax ID is stored in the Shopify customer profile, which Shopify automatically surfaces as a "Note" in the Customer section of the order. (Comment #4, #6)
- No manual action or third-party app is involved — Shopify natively carries this data into the order.
- Support escalates to L3 with collaborator access to the store (account ID: 32f69ae8-db0f-407e-973c-e4e64cefdcee). (Comment #8)

### Phase 3: Engineering Assessment and ETA (2026-06-03 to 2026-06-18)
