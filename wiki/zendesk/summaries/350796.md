## Open Issues

1. **WooCommerce Composite Products not imported into WSS** — Plugin only supports simple and variable WooCommerce product types. Customer's five "Create Your Own" gift-box products are Composite Products (using the WooCommerce Composite Products extension); when an order contains one, the product fails to import and the order does not appear in the app, blocking label generation entirely. Workaround offered (manual edit each order in WooCommerce to remove the composite item) is operationally untenable. Customer has held off on rebuilding products as a workaround pending engineering confirmation. Blocked: engineering. Severity: high (mandatory product type for customer's business model — gift boxes are their primary product; customer subscribed for 6+ months unable to ship these orders via the plugin; Yearly Popular plan paid). Area: product-management. (Comments #4, #10, #27, #29, #37, #43)

2. **Compatibility with WooCommerce Product Bundles / Product Add-Ons plugins not tested** — Customer asked twice whether either of these alternative product extensions would work with WSS as a workaround. Support response: "we have not tested" / "no comment on compatibility". Customer is blocked on whether to invest in rebuilding their store on a different product extension. Blocked: customer (waiting on PluginHive compatibility statement). Severity: medium (decision blocker for customer's workaround path). Area: product-management. (Comments #25, #39, #40)

3. **Multi-query email delivery suspected lost** — Customer reported sending 4 queries on a Thursday but support received only 1; tag `system_email_notification_failure` is present on the ticket. Area: other. (Comments #4, #5)

## Resolved Issues

1. **Country of Origin value rejected ('Canada' vs ISO code 'CA')** — Customer had set Country of Origin to the country name "Canada" instead of the required ISO-2 code `CA`, causing UPS rate/label failures. Touseef updated the affected product records in the app to use `CA` on 2025-09-24. (Comment #3)

2. **Address-correction suggestion misinterpreted as blocker** — Plugin's address validation surfaced a correction suggestion that the customer interpreted as an error. Touseef clarified it is informational and can be ignored when the address is verified correct. Resolved 2025-09-24. (Comments #1, #3)
