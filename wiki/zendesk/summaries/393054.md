# Ticket #393054 — TNT Australia Intermittent Rate Failure at Checkout Due to Pickup Slot Capacity

- **Customer**: Danielle Robertson (robertsonweb.com.au — web developer managing sieves.net.au store)
- **Product**: WooCommerce Multi Carrier Shipping (cross-product; tagged woocommerce_shipping_services)
- **Duration**: 2026-06-01 to 2026-06-18 (18 days, ongoing)

## Timeline & Key Phases

### Phase 1: Recurring Rate Failure Escalation (2026-06-01)
- Customer Danielle Robertson re-opened the issue, referencing a prior ticket (384139) and prior agent reply from May 8
- Her end-client (sieves.net.au / Dave Dick) had customers unable to complete checkout due to no shipping options appearing
- Support confirmed TNT Australia rates were returning at time of investigation (Comment #3)
- Root cause identified: TNT Australia's API blocks rate retrieval once daily pickup cutoff time is reached or pickup slots are fully booked for the day (Comment #4)
- Flat rate fallback was suggested as a workaround; customer rejected it — product sizes make flat rates impractical and commercially unviable (Comments #5, #6)

### Phase 2: Fallback Rates Feature Deployed (2026-06-04 to 2026-06-08)
- Engineering shipped a Fallback Rate option for TNT Australia services (Comment #8)
- Support communicated the feature on June 8: configurable fixed fallback rate per TNT service, displayed when live rates fail (Comment #9)
- Also communicated that a more permanent fix (automatic advancement to next available pickup slot) is in progress (Comment #9)

### Phase 3: Customer Confirmation and Pending Final Fix (2026-06-17 to 2026-06-18)
- Customer confirmed fallback rates are configured and working — no checkout failures since (Comment #11)
- Customer flagged that fallback rates are often inaccurate for their variable-size products, treating this as a temporary workaround (Comment #11)
- Engineering set internal ETA for permanent fix: on or before July 7, 2026 (Comment #15)

## Open Issues

1. **TNT Australia Automatic Pickup Slot Advancement Not Yet Deployed** — When TNT's API blocks rate retrieval due to daily pickup cutoff or full slot capacity, the app currently falls back to a fixed rate rather than automatically advancing the collection request to the next available pickup day or slot. The permanent fix that would retrieve live rates without manual intervention is still in progress. Blocked: customer (accuracy of displayed rates), engineering (feature in progress). Severity: active engineering commitment with hard ETA (July 7); customer is tolerating workaround but explicitly waiting for resolution. Area: rate-shopping. (Comment #9, #15)

## Resolved Issues

1. **TNT Australia Checkout Blocked When Live Rates Unavailable** — Introduced a configurable Fallback Rate option per TNT service. When live rates cannot be retrieved, the configured fallback rate is shown, preventing a blank shipping section at checkout. Customer confirmed this resolved the immediate checkout-blocking problem. (Comment #8, #11)
